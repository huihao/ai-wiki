# Tesla

## Summary

Tesla is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company founded in 2003. Beyond its well-known electric cars (Model S, 3, X, Y, Cybertruck), Tesla is a significant player in AI and machine learning through its Full Self-Driving (FSD) autonomous driving system and the Dojo supercomputer designed for training neural networks on video data. Tesla's AI division, led by notable engineers and researchers, has contributed to large-scale computer vision and end-to-end neural network approaches for autonomous driving.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: Organization (automotive, energy, AI)
- **Founded**: 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning; Elon Musk joined as chairman/CEO
- **Notable for**: Electric vehicles, Full Self-Driving (FSD) system, Dojo supercomputer, Gigafactories
- **Headquarters**: Austin, Texas, USA
- **Other facts**: Tesla's FSD system uses a vision-only approach (removed radar/lidar); the Dojo supercomputer is custom-designed for training on massive video datasets; Tesla Energy operates solar and Powerwall/Powerpack battery storage systems
