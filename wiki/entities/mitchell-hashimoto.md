# Mitchell Hashimoto

## Summary

Co-founder of HashiCorp and credited with coining the term "Harness Engineering" to describe the practice of building infrastructure around AI agents to make them reliable and productive. His work in infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, Vault, Consul) provides a conceptual foundation for treating AI agent orchestration as an infrastructure problem.
