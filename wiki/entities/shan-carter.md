# Shan Carter

## Summary

Shan Carter is a data journalist and visualization specialist who served as a key editor and contributor to Distill, the influential machine learning publication known for its interactive, visual explanations of ML concepts. He is known for his work on activation atlases and has been instrumental in shaping Distill's distinctive approach to visual communication of machine learning research.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Visualization specialist, editor
- **Affiliation**: Google Brain (formerly), New York Times (formerly)
- **Notable work**: Co-author of "Activation Atlases" (Distill, 2019), Distill editor
- **Research areas**: Data visualization, neural network visualization, interactive journalism
- **Impact**: Shaped Distill's visual communication style; activation atlases became a key interpretability tool
