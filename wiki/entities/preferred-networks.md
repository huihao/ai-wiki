# Preferred Networks

## Summary

Preferred Networks (PFN) is a Japanese AI startup and the creator of the Chainer deep learning framework, which significantly influenced PyTorch's design philosophy. Based in Tokyo, Japan.

## Key Attributes

- Creator of Chainer, a pioneering dynamic-graph deep learning framework
- Chainer's "define-by-run" approach directly inspired PyTorch's design
- Tokyo-based AI company focused on manufacturing, mobility, and bio/AI
- Key contributors to PyTorch ecosystem
