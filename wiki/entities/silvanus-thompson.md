# Silvanus P. Thompson

## Summary

Mathematics teacher and author of the classic textbook "Calculus Made Easy" (1910), known for making calculus accessible through intuitive explanations and everyday analogies.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Mathematics teacher / author
- **Notable Work**: Calculus Made Easy (1910)
- **Style**: Accessible, using everyday analogies to explain mathematical concepts
