# DeepMind

## Summary
DeepMind is Google's AI research division, originally founded in London in 2010 and acquired by Google in 2014. It merged with Google Brain in 2023 to form Google DeepMind. The organization is known for landmark achievements including AlphaGo (defeating the world Go champion in 2016), AlphaFold (solving the protein structure prediction problem), and contributing to the Gemini family of multimodal AI models. DeepMind's work spans reinforcement learning, neuroscience-inspired AI, and frontier model research.
