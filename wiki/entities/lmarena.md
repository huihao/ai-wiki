# LMArena

Platform for comparing and benchmarking frontier AI models through crowdsourced evaluation. Enables side-by-side chat with multiple LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) and provides leaderboards based on real user preferences.

## Features

- Side-by-side model comparison: chat with multiple AI models simultaneously
- Crowdsourced benchmarks: rankings derived from real user interactions
- Comprehensive leaderboards: tracks over 300 models across multiple evaluation dimensions
- Research contribution: user conversations shared publicly to support AI research

## Privacy Policy

Users must accept that conversations and personal information are:
- Disclosed to relevant AI providers
- May be publicly disclosed for research purposes
- Users advised not to submit sensitive information
