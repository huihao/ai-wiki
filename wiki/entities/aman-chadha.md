# Aman Chadha

## Summary

Aman Chadha is a machine learning researcher and educator who maintains "Aman's AI Journal" (aman.ai), a comprehensive collection of primers covering AI fundamentals and mathematics for deep learning. His materials serve as course notes for Stanford AI and Deep Learning classes.
