# Andrew Carr

## Summary

Andrew Carr is a machine learning researcher who confirmed the use of Maximal Update Parameterization (muP) at OpenAI (formerly). muP is a technique that ensures training dynamics transfer across model widths, enabling hyperparameter tuning on small proxy models that generalize to much larger models. His confirmation provides practical evidence that muP was adopted in production at frontier AI labs.
