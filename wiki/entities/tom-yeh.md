# Tom Yeh

## Summary
Tom Yeh is a professor known for the "AI by Hand" series, which teaches machine learning concepts through step-by-step hand calculations. His series on self-attention provides intuitive visual explanations of the Query-Key-Value mechanism and attention weight computation in Transformers, making complex deep learning concepts accessible through manual computation and visual diagrams.
