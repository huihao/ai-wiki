# Heidi Nance

> Librarian and guide creator for AI in Medicine resources at PCOM.

## Overview

Heidi Nance is a librarian at Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine (PCOM) who created and maintains the LibGuides collection on "Artificial Intelligence in Medicine and Healthcare." Her curated RSS feeds provide researchers and clinicians with valuable insights into the evolving landscape of AI in healthcare.

## Contributions

### AI Medicine LibGuide

Created comprehensive resource guide aggregating:
- 10+ journal RSS feeds (JAMA AI, Nature Machine Intelligence, Radiology AI)
- PubMed search feeds for AI-related medical literature
- Google AI Health updates
- Organized sections covering journals, databases, datasets, guidelines, research, publishing, training

### Coverage Areas

- AI applications in clinical decision support
- Medical informatics and information systems
- AI in medical imaging and radiology
- Natural language processing for medical texts
- Machine learning for health outcomes
- Health services research methodology

## Purpose

Provides centralized access to latest AI healthcare developments for:
- Medical researchers tracking emerging technologies
- Clinicians evaluating AI tools
- Students learning medical informatics
- Healthcare institutions adopting AI solutions
