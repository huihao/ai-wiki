# David Silver

## Summary

David Silver is a pioneering reinforcement learning researcher whose RL Course lectures (covering model-free prediction, model-free control, and planning by dynamic programming) are widely used as foundational educational resources. His course materials are studied by ML practitioners worldwide, including as part of structured deep learning study notes at ickma.dev.

## Key Attributes

- Pioneer in reinforcement learning research
- Creator of the widely-used RL Course (Lectures 3-5 referenced in educational notes)
- Known for foundational work on model-free prediction, model-free control, and dynamic programming
