# Alibaba Cloud

## Summary
Alibaba Cloud (also known as Aliyun) is the cloud computing arm of Alibaba Group and the largest cloud provider in China and the Asia-Pacific region. It offers a full stack of cloud services including compute, storage, databases, and AI/ML platforms. Alibaba Cloud has developed proprietary AI models such as Qwen (Tongyi Qianwen), a family of open-source large language models that have gained significant traction in the global AI community.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: cloud provider and AI platform
- **Notable for**: leading cloud provider in China; developer of the Qwen family of open-source LLMs
- **Founded**: 2009 (as Aliyun); rebranded as Alibaba Cloud in 2016
- **Parent company**: Alibaba Group
- **Headquarters**: Hangzhou, China
