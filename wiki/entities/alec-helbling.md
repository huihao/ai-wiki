# Alec Helbling

## Summary

ML PhD student at Georgia Tech advised by Polo Chau, supported by NSF GRFP. Research focuses on interpreting, visualizing, and guiding generative models, particularly for images and video. Incorporates tools from data visualization and HCI.

## Research Areas

- **Generative Models**: Image and video generation
- **Multimodal ML**: Vision-language models
- **ML Interpretability**: Understanding model behavior
- **Visualization**: Interactive tools for explaining ML

## Key Projects

- **ConceptAttention**: Method for interpreting models
- **Diffusion Explorer**: Visualization tool for diffusion models
- **ManimML**: Visualization framework for ML concepts
- **PrefGen**: Guiding generative model behavior

## Affiliations

- **Current**: Georgia Tech PhD student (Polo Chau advisor)
- **Apple**: Responsible AI team (vision-language models)
- **Adobe Firefly**: Text-to-image model safety
- **IBM Research**: Natural language summarization
- **NASA JPL**: Interactive visual tools for space science
- **University of Pittsburgh**: Computational biology

## Interests

Generative models, multimodal machine learning, ML interpretability, interactive visualization, HCI tools.
