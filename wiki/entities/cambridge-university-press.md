# Cambridge University Press

## Summary

Cambridge University Press is the publishing business of the University of Cambridge, one of the world's oldest and largest academic publishers. It publishes books, journals, and digital products across science, technology, humanities, and social sciences.

## Key Attributes

- Part of the University of Cambridge, founded 1534
- Published key ML textbooks including Dive into Deep Learning (D2L) and Introduction to Applied Linear Algebra
- One of the largest academic publishers globally
