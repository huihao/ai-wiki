# BAIR

## Summary

Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) is the AI research lab at UC Berkeley. It brings together researchers in computer vision, machine learning, natural language processing, planning, control, and robotics. BAIR operates one of the longest-running academic AI research blogs (since 2017) and has produced influential open-source tools and frameworks including Ray.

  - Soft Actor-Critic — widely used RL algorithm
