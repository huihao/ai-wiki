# Paul Rosenbloom

## Summary

Paul Rosenbloom is a computer scientist at University of Southern California and co-creator of the SOAR cognitive architecture (1987) with Allen Newell and John Laird. SOAR introduced the separation of working memory (declarative knowledge) from long-term procedural memory (production rules), a design pattern that anticipated modern agentic AI memory systems.
