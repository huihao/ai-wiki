# Leslie Lamport
## Summary
Pioneering computer scientist known for foundational work in distributed systems, including logical clocks, the happens-before relation, and the Paxos consensus algorithm. His most cited paper introduces logical clocks for ordering events in distributed systems.
## Key Attributes
- **Type**: Computer scientist
- **Known for**: Logical clocks, Paxos, LaTeX, TLA+
- **Most cited paper**: "Time, Clocks, and the Ordering of Events in a Distributed System"
