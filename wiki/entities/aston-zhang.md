# Aston Zhang

## Summary

Aston Zhang is a machine learning researcher and primary author of "Dive into Deep Learning" (D2L), an interactive, open-source deep learning textbook adopted at 500 universities across 70 countries and published by Cambridge University Press in 2023. The book is notable for its executable Jupyter notebook format covering deep learning from fundamentals to advanced topics.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Researcher, primary author
- **Affiliation**: Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- **Notable work**: Primary author of "Dive into Deep Learning" (D2L)
- **D2L role**: Lead author of the interactive deep learning textbook
- **Co-authors**: Mu Li, Zachary C. Lipton, Alexander J. Smola
