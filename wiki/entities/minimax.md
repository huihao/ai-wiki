# MiniMax

## Summary
MiniMax is a Chinese AI company founded in 2021, developing large language models and multimodal AI systems. The company is known for its multimodal foundation models and has gained attention for its Hailuo AI platform, which offers video generation and other creative AI capabilities alongside text and speech models.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: AI company / organization
- **Founded**: 2021
- **Headquarters**: Shanghai, China
- **Notable for**: Multimodal AI models combining text, speech, and video generation
- **Key products**: Hailuo AI (video generation), MiniMax-Text (LLM), MiniMax-V (vision model)
- **Funding**: Backed by major Chinese investors including Tencent and Alibaba-affiliated funds
