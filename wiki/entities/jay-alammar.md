# Jay Alammar

## Summary

Educator and visual explainer in machine learning and NLP. Best known for "The Illustrated" blog series — visual, intuitive breakdowns of complex ML concepts. His work has been incorporated into curricula at Stanford, Harvard, MIT, Princeton, and CMU. He has expanded his Transformer post into a book and short courses.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: ML educator / author / visual explainer
- **Notable works**:
  - *The Illustrated Transformer* (2018) — canonical visual intro to Transformers
  - *The Illustrated GPT-2* — visual guide to decoder-only language models
  - *The Illustrated BERT* — visual guide to bidirectional encoders
  - *LLM Book* (llm-book.com) — expanded book version of the Transformer post
- **Style**: Heavy use of diagrams, step-by-step visual walkthroughs, accessible to non-experts
