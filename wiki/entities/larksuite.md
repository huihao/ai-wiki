# Lark Suite

## Summary

Lark Suite (by ByteDance) is a collaborative productivity platform combining messaging, documents, calendar, and video conferencing, with AI-powered agent capabilities for workflow automation.

## Key Attributes

- Developed by ByteDance
- Combines messaging, docs, video, and calendar in one platform
- Offers Agent Skills for AI-powered workflow automation
- Competitor to Slack, Microsoft Teams, and Google Workspace
