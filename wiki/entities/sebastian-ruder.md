# Sebastian Ruder

## Summary

NLP researcher and author known for his comprehensive survey of gradient descent optimization algorithms (2016, arXiv:1609.04747), which has become one of the most widely referenced resources for understanding deep learning optimizers. His blog ruder.io covers optimization, transfer learning, and NLP topics. Also known for work on cross-lingual transfer learning and multi-task learning.
