# Ameet Talwalkar

## Definition

Professor at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) specializing in machine learning research. Research areas include federated learning, AutoML, interpretable machine learning, foundations of machine learning, neural architecture search, hyperparameter optimization, and distributed systems. Co-author of "Foundations of Machine Learning" textbook (MIT Press, 2012 & 2018).
