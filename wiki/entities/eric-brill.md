# Eric Brill

## Summary
Eric Brill is a researcher in natural language processing, known for his foundational work on transformation-based learning (also called Brill tagging), a method for part-of-speech tagging and other NLP tasks. His work in the 1990s and early 2000s contributed significantly to statistical approaches in NLP before the deep learning era.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: person
- **Notable for**: Developing Brill tagging (transformation-based learning) for NLP tasks
- **Focus areas**: Natural language processing, part-of-speech tagging, statistical NLP
- **Other facts**: His transformation-based error-driven learning approach was influential in early NLP systems
