# Amit Patel

## Summary

Amit Patel is the creator of Red Blob Games, a long-running educational website (since 1995) featuring interactive visual explanations of math and algorithms for game development. He has been contributing to game development education since 1990, with his current interactive tutorial style refined since 2007.

## Key Achievements

- **Red Blob Games**: Free, ad-free educational website since 1995
- Interactive tutorial style refined over 12+ years
- Comprehensive coverage: pathfinding, map generation, probability, geometry
- Collaborated with Peter Norvig on AI textbook diagrams
- Worked on multiple game projects: Realm of the Mad God, Galactic Assault Squad

## Projects & Collaborations
