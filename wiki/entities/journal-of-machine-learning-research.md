# Journal of Machine Learning Research

## Summary

The Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR) is a premier open-access academic journal in machine learning, established in 2000. It publishes high-quality scholarly articles across all areas of ML and is widely regarded as one of the top venues in the field alongside NeurIPS, ICML, and COLT. All papers are freely available online, with final versions published electronically immediately upon acceptance.

## Key Attributes

- **Full name**: Journal of Machine Learning Research
- **Established**: 2000
- **Publishing model**: Fully open-access, electronic-first
- **Publisher**: Microtome Publishing (paper volumes); formerly MIT Press (through 2004)
- **ISSN**: 1533-7928 (electronic); 1532-4435 (print)
- **Current volume**: Volume 27 (as of 2025–2026)
- **Scope**: All areas of machine learning — theory, algorithms, applications, and open-source software
- **Notable features**:
  - Rapid electronic publication upon acceptance
  - Machine Learning Open Source Software (MLOSS) track
  - Special issues (e.g., climate change in 2022)
