# Ian Goodfellow

## Summary

Ian Goodfellow is a machine learning researcher known for creating Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and co-authoring the Deep Learning textbook. He has worked at Google Brain and Apple, contributing to fundamental advances in generative models, adversarial training, and deep learning theory.

## Key Attributes

- **Notable work**: Creator of GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks)
- **Textbook**: Co-authored "Deep Learning" (2016) with Bengio and Courville
- **Affiliations**: Google Brain, Apple, OpenAI (former)
- **Research**: Generative models, adversarial robustness, deep learning theory
