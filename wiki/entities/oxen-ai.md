# Oxen.ai

## Summary

Oxen.ai is an AI platform providing a model library and end-to-end AI lifecycle management. It hosts 143 models across multiple modalities (text, image, video, audio, embeddings) from major AI developers including OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Qwen, ByteDance, and others. Oxen.ai positions itself as more than a model marketplace, offering tools for model experimentation, fine-tuning, and team collaboration.

## Key Attributes

- **Founded**: Oxen Labs, Inc.
- **Platform focus**: Model library + AI lifecycle management
- **Model catalog**: 143 models from 20+ developers
- **Modalities**: Text, image, video, audio, embeddings
- **Features**: Try models, share outputs with team, fine-tuning support
- **Business model**: Pay-per-use (tokens, images, seconds of video)
