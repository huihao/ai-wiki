# Mikolov et al.
## Summary
Research team at Google who introduced the Word2Vec model in 2013, consisting of Tomas Mikolov and colleagues. Their work on distributed representations of words revolutionized NLP by showing that dense word embeddings could capture semantic relationships.
## Key Attributes
- **Affiliation**: Google (at time of publication)
- **Key papers**: "Efficient Estimation of Word Representations in Vector Space", "Distributed Representations of Words and Phrases"
- **Key contribution**: Word2Vec (CBOW and Skip-gram models)
