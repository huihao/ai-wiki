# Adam Paszke

## Summary

Adam Paszke is a software engineer and machine learning researcher best known as the co-creator of PyTorch, the dominant deep learning framework used in research and industry. He joined Facebook AI Research (FAIR) as an intern in 2016 at Soumith Chintala's invitation, where he built the first version of PyTorch from scratch by forking LuaTorch and designing a modern Pythonic interface with dynamic computation graphs.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Software engineer, framework creator
- **Affiliation**: Google Brain (formerly), Meta AI / FAIR (formerly)
- **Notable work**: Co-creator of PyTorch
- **Key contribution**: Wrote the first version of PyTorch as an intern at FAIR in 2016
- **Research areas**: Automatic differentiation, deep learning frameworks, compiler design
- **Later work**: Involved in 5 compiler design iterations for PyTorch's torch.compile
