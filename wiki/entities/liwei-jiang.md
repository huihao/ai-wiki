# Liwei Jiang

## Summary

Liwei Jiang is a researcher known for work on AI safety and large language model evaluation. She served as the lead author of "Artificial Hivemind," a NeurIPS 2025 Best Paper Award winner. The paper introduced Infinity-Chat, a dataset of 26K diverse open-ended queries with 31K human annotations, revealing both intra-model repetition and inter-model homogeneity in LLM outputs. The work raised critical concerns about how repeated exposure to homogeneous LLM-generated content could homogenize human thought long-term, threatening creativity and value plurality.

## Key Attributes
- Lead author of "Artificial Hivemind" (NeurIPS 2025 Best Paper Award)
- Research areas include AI safety, natural language processing, and LLM evaluation
- Created Infinity-Chat dataset (26K queries, 31K human annotations) for studying open-ended generation
- Work highlights risks of LLM homogeneity to human creativity and value diversity
