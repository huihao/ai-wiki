# Vincent Dumoulin

## Summary

Vincent Dumoulin is a machine learning researcher known for his contributions to neural network interpretability, specifically on the topic of learned conditioning mechanisms in neural networks. He is a contributor to Distill and has published research exploring how neural networks can learn to selectively process and condition on different types of information, advancing understanding of network internals.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Researcher
- **Affiliation**: Mila (Montreal Institute for Learning Algorithms), Universite de Montreal
- **Notable work**: Distill contributor on conditioning mechanisms
- **Research areas**: Deep learning theory, conditioning mechanisms, neural network interpretability, representation learning
- **Co-author with**: Yoshua Bengio (on conditioning mechanisms article)
