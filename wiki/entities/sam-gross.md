# Sam Gross

## Summary

Sam Gross is a software engineer who made significant contributions to PyTorch's development as a full-time member of the core team at Facebook AI Research (FAIR). He joined during PyTorch's early creation phase and contributed to key infrastructure components including data loading systems. He has since worked on other ML systems at Meta and in the broader AI research community.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Software engineer, core framework developer
- **Affiliation**: Meta AI / FAIR (formerly)
- **Notable work**: Core PyTorch developer, contributed to data loading systems
- **Key contribution**: Joined full-time during PyTorch's early creation (mid-2016), contributed to PyTorch data loading with Tongzhou Wang
- **FAIR team**: Part of the original 3-person core team alongside Soumith Chintala and Gregory Chanan
