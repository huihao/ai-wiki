# xAI

## Summary

xAI is an artificial intelligence company founded by Elon Musk in 2023. It develops large language models, most notably the Grok family of models. xAI operates the Colossus supercomputer cluster and aims to develop AI systems that can understand the true nature of the universe. Grok models are integrated into the X (formerly Twitter) platform.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: AI company / research organization
- **Notable for**: developing the Grok family of large language models
- **Other facts**: founded in March 2023; headquartered in the San Francisco Bay Area; built the Colossus supercomputer in Memphis, Tennessee; Grok models are available via the X platform and API
