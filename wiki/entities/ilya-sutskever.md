# Ilya Sutskever

## Summary

Co-founder and former chief scientist of OpenAI, and a central figure in the deep learning revolution. Known for co-authoring the ImageNet-winning AlexNet, the Sequence to Sequence learning paper, and curating influential reading lists for the field.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Co-founder, former Chief Scientist at OpenAI
- **Education**: PhD from University of Toronto under Geoffrey Hinton
- **Key work**: AlexNet (2012), Seq2Seq (2014), GPT series contributions
- **Current focus**: Founded Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI) in 2024
