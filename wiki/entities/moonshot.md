# Moonshot

## Summary
Moonshot AI (also known as Kimi AI) is a Chinese AI company founded in 2023, known for developing the Kimi series of large language models with a focus on long-context understanding. The company has been notable for pushing context window boundaries, with Kimi models supporting very large context windows early on.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: AI company / organization
- **Founded**: 2023
- **Headquarters**: Beijing, China
- **Notable for**: Early focus on long-context LLMs; the Kimi chatbot gained significant popularity in China
- **Key models**: Kimi (long-context LLM series)
- **Backed by**: Major Chinese investors including Alibaba
