# Codeforces

Popular competitive programming platform hosting regular competitions attracting tens of thousands of participants worldwide.

## Competition Format

Participants receive series of long problem descriptions and few hours to write programs to solve them.

**Typical problems**:
- Placing roads and buildings within constraints
- Creating strategies to win custom board games

**Ranking**: Mainly based on number of problems solved

## Use Cases

- Skill showcase and experience gain
- Recruiting tool for companies
- Similar problems in software engineer hiring processes

## AlphaCode Evaluation

[[AlphaCode]] evaluated on 10 recent Codeforces contests (each newer than training data). Platform granted permission for simulated participation. AlphaCode placed at approximately median competitor level.

## Significance

Domain where solving problems through shortcuts (duplicating solutions, trying every algorithm) is impossible. Models must create novel and interesting solutions.
