# Zhipu

## Summary

Zhipu AI is a Chinese artificial intelligence company founded in 2019 as a spin-off from Tsinghua University's KEG Lab. It develops large language models including the GLM (General Language Model) series and the ChatGLM family. Zhipu is one of China's leading AI startups and has been recognized in benchmark evaluations comparing frontier AI models.

- **Type**: AI company / research organization

- **Other facts**: founded in 2019 as a Tsinghua University spin-off; backed by major Chinese technology investors; develops both open-source and commercial LLM products; operates the BigModel platform for AI application development
