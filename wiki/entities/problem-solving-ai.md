# Problem-Solving AI

## Summary
Problem-solving AI refers to artificial intelligence systems designed to tackle complex, multi-step problems that require critical thinking, logical reasoning, and algorithmic creativity — tasks traditionally associated with human intelligence. A landmark milestone was DeepMind's AlphaCode, which in 2022 achieved a rank within the top 54% of human participants in competitive programming contests on Codeforces, marking the first time an AI system reached competitive-level performance in programming competitions. These systems generate novel solutions rather than duplicating existing code, demonstrating the potential of deep learning for tasks requiring genuine reasoning.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: concept / research direction
- **Notable for**: AlphaCode achieved median competitive programmer performance on Codeforces
- **Key milestone**: Published on the cover of Science (December 2022)
- **Approach**: Transformer-based models generating code at massive scale, then filtering and reranking candidates
- **Benchmark**: Codeforces competitive programming contests
