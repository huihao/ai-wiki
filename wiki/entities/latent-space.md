# Latent Space

## Summary

Latent Space is a media brand and community focused on AI Engineering. It produces podcasts, essays, reading lists, and events (notably the AI Engineer Summit). The brand is associated with practitioners [[shawn-wang]] (swyx) and [[alessio-fanelli]], who interview researchers, founders, and engineers building with LLMs.

- **Format**: Podcast, newsletter, community, conference
- **Focus**: Practical AI Engineering — what working engineers need to know
- **Notable outputs**: Annual "Best of" series, [[ai-engineering|The 2025 AI Engineering Reading List]], paper club guides
- **Events**: AI Engineer Summit (NYC, Feb 2025)
