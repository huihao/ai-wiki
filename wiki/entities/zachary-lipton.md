# Zachary Lipton

## Summary

Zachary C. Lipton is a machine learning researcher, professor at Carnegie Mellon University, and co-author of "Dive into Deep Learning" (D2L), an interactive deep learning textbook adopted at 500 universities across 70 countries. He is known for his work on the medical applications of deep learning, the limitations of machine learning models, and principled approaches to AI fairness and interpretability.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Professor, researcher, textbook author
- **Affiliation**: Carnegie Mellon University (CMU)
- **Notable work**: Co-authored "Dive into Deep Learning" (D2L)
- **Research areas**: Medical AI, fairness in ML, interpretability, deep learning theory
- **Co-authors**: Aston Zhang, Mu Li, Alexander J. Smola
