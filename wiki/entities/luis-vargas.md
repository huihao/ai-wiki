# Luis Vargas

## Summary

Luis Vargas is a Microsoft VP of AI who was part of the team behind the Phi-3 family of small language models (SLMs). The Phi-3 project demonstrated that smaller models trained on high-quality, carefully curated data could outperform models twice their size on language, coding, and math benchmarks.

## Key Attributes

- Microsoft VP of AI
- Part of the Phi-3 small language model development team
