# Allen Newell

## Summary

Allen Newell (1927–1992) was a computer scientist and cognitive psychologist at Carnegie Mellon University. Co-creator of the SOAR cognitive architecture (1987) with Paul Rosenbloom and John Laird, which pioneered the separation of working memory from long-term procedural memory in artificial intelligence systems.

## Key Attributes

- **Known for**: SOAR cognitive architecture, contributions to AI and cognitive science
- **Affiliation**: Carnegie Mellon University
