# Claude Opus

## Summary

Anthropic's flagship model, offering the highest reasoning capability and most sophisticated performance among the Claude family. Best suited for complex analysis, nuanced writing, and tasks requiring deep understanding, at the trade-off of higher latency and cost.

## Key Attributes

- **Developer**: Anthropic
- **Positioning**: Most capable model in the Claude family (Opus > Sonnet > Haiku)
- **Strengths**: Deep multi-step reasoning, highest quality output, nuanced analysis
- **Model ID**: `claude-opus-4-7`
- **Use Cases**: Complex code generation, multi-step problem solving, nuanced analysis, advanced research tasks
- **API Access**: Available through Anthropic's Messages API
