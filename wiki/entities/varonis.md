# Varonis

## Summary

Varonis is a cybersecurity company specializing in data security, including threat detection, data loss prevention, and ransomware protection. They publish educational blog content on topics including malware analysis, reverse engineering, and incident response. Their platform provides data risk assessments and automated remediation for enterprise data security.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: cybersecurity company (public, founded 2005)
- **Notable for**: data-centric security platform that monitors and protects enterprise file systems, email, and cloud data
- **Other facts**: headquartered in New York City; founded by Guy and Kfir Eldad; publishes detailed technical blog posts on malware unpacking and reverse engineering techniques
