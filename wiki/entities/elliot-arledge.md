# Elliot Arledge

## Summary

Elliot Arledge is a GPU optimization expert and creator of MegaQwen, a CUDA megakernel implementation that achieved 530 tokens/second on an RTX 3090 GPU. His work on persistent kernels and decode optimization pushed the boundaries of LLM inference speed, inspiring further optimizations by others such as Alpin Dale who achieved 1,000+ tok/s on an RTX 5090.

## Key Attributes

- GPU optimization expert
- Creator of MegaQwen (530 tok/s on RTX 3090)
- Pioneer in persistent CUDA kernel approaches for LLM inference
- Work inspired further decode optimization efforts in the community
