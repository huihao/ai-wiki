# Thoughtworks

## Summary

Thoughtworks is a global technology consultancy known for championing agile development, continuous delivery, DevOps, and technology excellence. The company has been influential in shaping modern software engineering practices and has published extensively on topics including evolutionary architecture, domain-driven design, and more recently, AI-assisted software development. Their "Exploring Gen AI" series on martinfowler.com explores the practical impact of AI on software development workflows.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: Global technology consultancy
- **Founded**: 1993 by Roy Singham
- **Notable for**: Pioneering agile and XP practices; publishing thought leadership on martinfowler.com; contributing to software engineering methodology
- **Headquarters**: Chicago, Illinois, USA
- **Other facts**: Martin Fowler is Thoughtworks' Chief Scientist; the company has been a key voice in context engineering, AI-assisted development, and continuous delivery; authors include Birgitta Boeckeler and Bharani Subramaniam
