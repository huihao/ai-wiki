# VirusTotal
## Summary
A widely-used online service that analyzes files and URLs for malware by scanning with multiple antivirus engines. Provides detection rates across dozens of engines, making it a standard tool for evaluating AV evasion techniques in security research.
## Key Attributes
- **Type**: Malware scanning service
- **Owner**: Google (acquired 2012)
- **Key feature**: Multi-engine scanning (50+ AV engines)
