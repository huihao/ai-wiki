# Tmall

## Summary

Tmall is Alibaba Group's business-to-consumer (B2C) online retail platform, one of the largest e-commerce websites in China. Launched in 2008 as a division of Taobao, Tmall focuses on brand-name goods and has become a major platform for international and domestic brands to reach Chinese consumers. Tmall hosts major shopping festivals including the annual Singles' Day (11.11) event.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: B2C e-commerce platform
- **Notable for**: being one of China's largest online retail platforms and hosting the Singles' Day shopping festival
- **Other facts**: launched in 2008 as Taobao Mall; rebranded to Tmall in 2012; part of Alibaba Group; hosts Singles' Day (11.11), which generates record-breaking annual sales; offers cross-border e-commerce through Tmall Global
