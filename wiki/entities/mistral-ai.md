# Mistral AI

## Summary

European AI lab and company known for high-performance open-weight language models. Mistral popularized Sliding Window Attention in open models and continues to iterate on efficient transformer architectures.

## Key Attributes

- **Founded**: 2023
- **Headquarters**: Paris, France
- **Key models**: Mistral 7B, Mixtral 8x7B (MoE), Mistral Small 3.1, Mistral Large 3
- **Architecture choices**: Standard GQA or MLA depending on model size
