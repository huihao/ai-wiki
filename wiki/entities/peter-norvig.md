# Peter Norvig

## Summary

Peter Norvig is a renowned AI researcher and co-author of "Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach," the standard AI textbook. He collaborated with Amit Patel on diagrams for the textbook, and Red Blob Games was created in part to support this collaboration. His work has been foundational to AI education worldwide.

## Key Attributes

- Renowned AI researcher
- Co-author of "Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach" (with Stuart Russell)
- Collaborated with Amit Patel on textbook diagrams
- Former Director of Research at Google
