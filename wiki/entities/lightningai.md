# Lightning AI

## Summary

Lightning AI (formerly PyTorch Lightning) is a company and open-source framework for building, training, and deploying ML models with simplified PyTorch code, founded by Luca Antiga and William Falcon.

## Key Attributes

- Creator of PyTorch Lightning framework
- Provides cloud-based ML development platform
- Simplifies distributed training, mixed precision, and deployment
- Founded by Luca Antiga (early PyTorch contributor) and William Falcon
