# Sumit Analyzen

## Summary

Sumit Analyzen is the author (along with Sumit Saha) of "The AI Coding Loop: How to Guide AI With Rules and Tests," published on freeCodeCamp. The article presents a practical 7-step workflow for shipping secure code with AI assistance: defining goals, writing rules, creating examples, identifying edge cases, building incrementally with runnable tests, and iterating on failures.

## Key Attributes
