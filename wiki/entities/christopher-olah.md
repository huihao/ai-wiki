# Christopher Olah

Researcher and blogger known for influential visual explanations of deep learning concepts. Creator of colah.github.io blog.

## Notable Work

### Understanding LSTM Networks (2015)

Classic blog post explaining Long Short-Term Memory networks:
- Visualized RNN chains and LSTM architecture
- Explained forget gate, input gate, output gate mechanics
- Discussed LSTM variants (peephole connections, coupled gates, GRU)
- Predicted attention mechanism as next major direction
- Referenced in countless deep learning courses and tutorials

### Other Contributions

- Neural networks, manifolds, and topology visualizations
- Conv nets modular perspective
- NLP representations with RNNs
- Worked at Google with Oriol Vinyals, Greg Corrado, Jon Shlens, Luke Vilnis, Ilya Sutskever

## Influence

- Blog posts widely cited in ML education
- Visual explanations set standard for pedagogical deep learning content
- Helped establish intuitive understanding approach to complex architectures
