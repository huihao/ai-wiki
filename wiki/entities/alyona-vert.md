# Alyona Vert

## Summary
Alyona Vert is an author at Turing Post, where she writes about emerging memory architectures for AI agents. Her work explores how agents can retain, retrieve, and leverage information across interactions through episodic memory, semantic memory, and other architectural approaches. Her article "7 Emerging Memory Architectures for AI Agents" is a notable survey covering frameworks such as AgeMem, Memex, MemRL, UMA, and Pancake, providing a structured overview of how memory systems are evolving to support longer-running, more capable AI agents.
