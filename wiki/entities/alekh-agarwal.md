# Alekh Agarwal

## Summary

Alekh Agarwal is a machine learning researcher specializing in reinforcement learning theory, contextual bandits, and statistical learning. He has held affiliations with Microsoft Research and has taught at the University of Washington and Columbia University. He served as Program Chair for NeurIPS 2022 and has received best/outstanding paper awards at NIPS 2015 and ICML 2022.

## Key Attributes

- **Field**: Reinforcement learning theory, contextual bandits, active learning, offline RL
- **Institutions**: Microsoft Research; University of Washington (teaching); Columbia University (teaching)
- **Key co-authors**: Sham Kakade, Nan Jiang, John Langford, Miroslav Dudík, Akshay Krishnamurthy
- **Awards**: Best paper award (NIPS 2015); Outstanding paper award (ICML 2022)
- **Service**: Program Chair, NeurIPS 2022; Senior Area Chair (NeurIPS 2019–2020); Area Chair (ICML, NeurIPS, COLT, AISTATS 2013–2020)
- **Notable works**:
  - *RL Theory Monograph* (with Nan Jiang and Sham Kakade)
  - *On the Theory of Policy Gradient Methods* (JMLR 2020)
  - *Adversarially Trained Actor Critic for Offline RL* (ICML 2022, outstanding paper)
  - *Fast Convergence of Regularized Learning in Games* (NIPS 2015, best paper)
