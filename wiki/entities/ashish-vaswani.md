# Ashish Vaswani

## Summary

Lead author of the seminal "Attention Is All You Need" paper (2017) that introduced the Transformer architecture, revolutionizing NLP and enabling modern LLMs like GPT, BERT, and PaLM.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Researcher
- **Affiliation**: Google Brain (at time of paper)
- **Notable Work**: "Attention Is All You Need" (2017)
- **Impact**: Transformer architecture is foundation of all modern LLMs
