# Meta AI

## Summary

Meta AI is the artificial intelligence research division of Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook), producing open-source models (LLaMA), research on RAG, and contributions to NLP and computer vision.

## Key Attributes

- Research division of Meta Platforms
- Creator of LLaMA family of open-source LLMs
- Published seminal RAG paper (2020) defining Retrieval-Augmented Generation
- Open-source AI research focused on NLP, CV, and speech
