# University of California, Berkeley

## Summary

UC Berkeley is a top public research university and a major center for AI/ML research, contributing to deep learning frameworks, reinforcement learning, and computer science education.

## Key Attributes

- Top-ranked public university for CS and AI research
- Home to BAIR (Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research)
- Major contributor to D2L (Dive into Deep Learning) textbook
- Birthplace of key ML innovations (Berkeley AI, RISELab, Sky Computing)
