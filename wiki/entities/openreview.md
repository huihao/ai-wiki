# OpenReview

## Summary

OpenReview is an open-access peer review and publication platform widely used in machine learning and AI. It hosts major conferences with transparent or double-blind review processes and makes accepted papers and reviews publicly accessible.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: Non-profit academic platform
- **Hosted conferences**: NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ACL, CVPR, and many others
- **Features**: Submission, review, discussion forums, meta-reviews, public access
