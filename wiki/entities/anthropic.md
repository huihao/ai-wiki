# Anthropic

## Summary

Anthropic is an AI safety company known for Claude, a family of large language models. It emphasizes AI safety research (Constitutional AI) and has developed both consumer products (Claude.ai) and developer tools including Claude Code, a terminal-based coding agent. Anthropic also created the Model Context Protocol (MCP), an open standard for AI tool integration. Anthropic's Claude models consistently rank among top performers in AI benchmarks, holding #2 and #3 positions on the 2026 leaderboard.

## Key Attributes

- **Founded**: 2021
- **Key products**: Claude (LLM family), Claude Code, Claude Desktop, MCP
- **Founders**: Dario and Daniela Amodei (former OpenAI)
- **Focus**: AI safety, Constitutional AI, interpretability
- **Notable models**: Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Claude 3 Opus, Claude 4.x series
- **Benchmark Performance**: #2 and #3 on AI leaderboard (2026)
- **Context Window**: Up to 200K-1M tokens
- **Pricing**: Mid-range ($3-$15 input, $15-$75 output per million tokens)
