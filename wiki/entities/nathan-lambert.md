# Nathan Lambert

## Summary

Nathan Lambert is an AI researcher and author specializing in reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF) and post-training for language models. He authored the comprehensive RLHF Book covering theory, implementation, and advanced research questions. The book is published online and in print by Manning Publications.

## Key Achievements

- **RLHF Book**: Comprehensive guide covering RLHF theory and practice (2026)
- Manning Publications print edition
- Course page with lecture videos (March 2026)
- Code examples library and RL cheatsheet
- Contributors include John Schulman (PPO author), Costa Huang, and others

## Book Evolution
