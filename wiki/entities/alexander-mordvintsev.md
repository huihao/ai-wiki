# Alexander Mordvintsev

## Summary

Alexander Mordvintsev is a machine learning researcher and artist best known as the creator of DeepDream, the neural network visualization technique that produced iconic psychedelic imagery and brought attention to how neural networks process visual information. He is a contributor to Distill, where he has published work on feature visualization and neural network interpretability, and works at Google.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Researcher, creative technologist
- **Affiliation**: Google
- **Notable work**: Creator of DeepDream (2015), Distill contributor
- **Research areas**: Neural network visualization, feature visualization, interpretability, computational art
- **Impact**: DeepDream popularized the concept of visualizing what neural networks learn, influencing both research and popular culture
