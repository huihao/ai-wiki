# Apple

## Summary

Apple is a multinational technology company known for consumer electronics and increasingly for on-device artificial intelligence. Apple's AI strategy emphasizes privacy-preserving, on-device inference through Core ML, the Neural Engine (Apple Silicon), and Apple Intelligence. Unlike cloud-first competitors, Apple optimizes models to run locally on iPhones, iPads, and Macs.

## Key Attributes

- **Founded**: 1976
- **Headquarters**: Cupertino, California
- **AI strategy**: On-device inference, privacy-first, Apple Intelligence
- **Key hardware**: Apple Silicon (M-series chips with Neural Engine), iPhone, Vision Pro
- **Key frameworks**: Core ML, MLX (open-source ML framework for Apple Silicon)
- **Notable AI products**: Siri, Apple Intelligence (iOS 18+), MLX
