# Xiaomi

## Summary

Xiaomi Corporation is a Chinese multinational technology company headquartered in Beijing, known for smartphones, smart home devices, and IoT products. Founded in 2010 by Lei Jun, Xiaomi has grown into one of the world's largest smartphone manufacturers by unit sales. The company has invested heavily in artificial intelligence and operates one of the world's largest consumer IoT platforms.
