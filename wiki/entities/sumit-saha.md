# Sumit Saha

## Summary

Sumit Saha is the co-author (along with Sumit Analyzen) of "The AI Coding Loop: How to Guide AI With Rules and Tests," published on freeCodeCamp. The article presents a disciplined 7-step workflow for shipping secure code with AI assistance, emphasizing that if you can't verify AI-generated code, you don't own it, and if you don't own it, you can't safely ship it.
