# IBM Research

## Summary

IBM Research is the research division of IBM, one of the world's oldest and largest corporate research labs, with breakthroughs in AI (Watson, Granite models), quantum computing, and materials science.

## Key Attributes

- Founded 1945, one of the largest corporate research organizations
- Multiple research labs worldwide
- Known for Watson AI platform and Granite foundation models
- Contributions to NLP summarization and enterprise AI
