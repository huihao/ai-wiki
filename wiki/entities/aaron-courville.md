# Aaron Courville

## Summary

Aaron Courville is a machine learning researcher and professor at Universite de Montreal, best known as co-author of the foundational "Deep Learning" textbook alongside Ian Goodfellow and Yoshua Bengio. He is a core member of MILA (Montreal Institute for Learning Algorithms) and has contributed to research on deep learning theory, generative models, and natural language processing.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Researcher, professor, textbook author
- **Affiliation**: Universite de Montreal, MILA (Montreal Institute for Learning Algorithms)
- **Notable work**: Co-authored "Deep Learning" (2016) with Ian Goodfellow and Yoshua Bengio - one of the most widely used deep learning textbooks globally
- **Research areas**: Deep learning theory, generative models, NLP, optimization
- **Co-authors**: Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio
