# Shay Moran

## Summary

Shay Moran is a researcher known for contributions to learning theory and online learning. He co-authored "Optimal Mistake Bounds for Transductive Online Learning," a NeurIPS 2025 Runner-Up paper that resolved a 30-year-old open problem. The work proved a tight mistake bound of theta(sqrt(d)) for transductive online learning, establishing a quadratic gap between transductive and standard online learning, using a sophisticated adversary strategy and an innovative "sparse encoding" hypothesis class construction.

- Contributions to understanding fundamental limits of learning algorithms
