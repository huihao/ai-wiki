# Kevin Wang

## Summary

Kevin Wang is a researcher recognized for pioneering work in deep reinforcement learning. He served as the lead author of "1000 Layer Networks for Self-Supervised RL," a NeurIPS 2025 Best Paper Award winner. The paper overturned the conventional assumption that RL cannot effectively train very deep networks by demonstrating that 1024-layer networks significantly boost self-supervised RL performance in goal-reaching tasks without requiring demonstrations or rewards, revealing depth as a critical scaling factor.

## Key Attributes
- Lead author of "1000 Layer Networks for Self-Supervised RL" (NeurIPS 2025 Best Paper Award)
- Research focus includes deep reinforcement learning and self-supervised learning
- Demonstrated that depth is a critical scaling factor for self-supervised RL
