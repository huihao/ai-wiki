# John Schulman

## Summary
John Schulman is a co-founder of OpenAI and the lead author of the Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) paper. He is widely recognized for his foundational contributions to reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF), which became the core alignment technique behind ChatGPT and subsequent large language models. He later joined Anthropic to continue working on AI safety.
