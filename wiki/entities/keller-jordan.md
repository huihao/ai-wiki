# Keller Jordan
## Summary
AI researcher and creator of the Muon (MomentUm Orthogonalized by Newton-Schulz) optimizer, which has gained popularity for its ability to boost training efficiency. Muon orthogonalizes momentum using Newton-Schulz iteration, improving spectral properties of weight updates.
