# Epoch Capability Index

## Summary
The Epoch Capability Index is a composite benchmark metric maintained by Epoch AI that aggregates results from over 50 individual AI benchmarks into a single unified score. It covers capabilities across mathematics, coding, language understanding, vision, and agentic action, providing a standardized way to compare the general capability of different AI models. The index is designed to track overall AI progress over time rather than performance on any single task.
