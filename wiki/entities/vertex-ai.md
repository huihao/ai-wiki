# Vertex AI

## Summary

Vertex AI is Google Cloud's managed AI platform for building, deploying, and scaling ML models, providing end-to-end MLOps tools including AutoML, custom training, and model serving.

## Key Attributes

- Google Cloud's unified AI/ML platform
- Supports PyTorch model deployment via TorchServe
- Provides AutoML, custom training, and model serving
- Integrates with BigQuery ML and other GCP services
- Enterprise-grade MLOps and model management
