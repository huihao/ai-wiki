# Google Research

## Summary

Google Research houses the federated learning and analytics team that develops foundational technologies for privacy-preserving AI systems. The team maintains a collection of open source projects and aims to invent technologies for a private-by-default ecosystem.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: Research organization
- **Affiliation**: Google
- **Team**: Federated learning and analytics team
- **Focus**: Strong privacy guarantees for AI and analytics systems
- **Inventions**: Federated learning, analytics, private aggregation, differential privacy
- **Mission**: Private-by-default ecosystem with transparent, verifiable privacy
- **Open Source**: Parfait collection (github.com/google-parfait)
- **Contact**: federated-feedback@google.com
