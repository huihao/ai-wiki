# Microsoft

## Summary

Microsoft is a technology corporation with significant investments in AI through its partnership with OpenAI, Azure AI services, and its own model research including the Phi family of small language models.

## Key Attributes

- **AI partnership**: OpenAI (major investor and cloud provider)
- **Key products**: Azure OpenAI Service, Copilot, Phi models
- **Research focus**: Small language models, multi-modal AI, productivity AI
- **Agent Skills**: Major provider with azure-skills (4.9M installs) and github-copilot-for-azure (1.7M installs)
