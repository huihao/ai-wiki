# Chip Huyen

## Summary

Machine learning engineer, author, and educator. Known for bridging the gap between ML research and production engineering. Author of *Designing Machine Learning Systems* (O'Reilly, 2022) and the upcoming *AI Engineering* (O'Reilly). Writes extensively on ML platform architecture, RAG systems, and generative AI deployment. Previously worked at Netflix, NVIDIA, and Snorkel AI.
