# Soumith Chintala

## Summary

ML researcher and framework developer who co-created PyTorch from first principles in 2016. Maintainer of Torch7 (LuaTorch) since April 2014. Documented PyTorch's design origins, inspirations, and philosophy of attributing ideas openly.

## Timeline

**2012:** Deep involvement in Torch7 (LuaTorch)

**April 2014:** Official maintainer status for Torch7 alongside Ronan Collobert, Clement Farabet, Koray Kavuckuoglu

**Late 2015 - Mid 2016:** Led refactoring of LuaTorch C backends (TH/THC, THNN/THCUNN) to be language-agnostic

**Mid 2016:** Invited [[adam-paszke]] for internship to build next version of LuaTorch → PyTorch genesis

**2016-2017:** Core PyTorch development team (~3 people at FAIR: Gregory Chanan, Trevor Killeen, Soumith Chintala)

**2017+:** Post-launch evolution: community design, growth, innovation, GPU code contributions (with NVIDIA and Meta)

**December 2023:** Published "PyTorch's design origins" documenting history, inspirations, and attribution philosophy
