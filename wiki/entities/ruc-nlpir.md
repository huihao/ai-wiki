# RUC-NLPIR

## Summary

Renmin University of China (RUC) NLPIR Lab is a research group focused on natural language processing and information retrieval. They created FlashRAG, a comprehensive toolkit for RAG research accepted at WWW 2025.

## Key Work

- **FlashRAG** — Python toolkit for RAG research with 23 baseline methods, 36 datasets
- **DomainRAG** — in-domain RAG dataset based on RUC enrollment data
