# Costa Huang

## Summary

Costa Huang is a contributor to Nathan Lambert's RLHF Book (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback), a comprehensive resource covering the origins, theory, and practical implementation of RLHF and post-training methodologies for language models. The book is published by Manning Publications and progresses from instruction tuning through reward modeling to advanced optimization algorithms like PPO and DPO.

## Key Attributes

- Contributor to the RLHF Book by Nathan Lambert
- Involved in RLHF and alignment research community
