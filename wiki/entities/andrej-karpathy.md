# Andrej Karpathy

## Summary

Andrej Karpathy is a prominent AI researcher and educator, formerly Director of AI at Tesla and co-founder of OpenAI. He is widely credited with coining the term "vibe coding" in early 2025 and later popularizing "agentic engineering" as the more disciplined successor. He advocates for LLM-maintained knowledge bases and architecture-driven AI coding.

## Key Attributes

- **Former roles**: Director of AI at Tesla, co-founder of OpenAI

- **Notable terms coined**: "vibe coding" (Feb 2025), "agentic engineering" (Feb 2026)
- **Key projects**: auto-research, LLM Knowledge Base architecture
