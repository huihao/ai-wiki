# Avi Chawla

## Summary

Avi Chawla is the author of "Anatomy of the .claude/ Folder," a comprehensive guide to Claude Code's configuration system published on his Daily Dose of DS blog. The guide explains the full structure of project-level and global .claude/ directories, covering CLAUDE.md, custom commands, skills, agents, and permissions, helping developers understand how to configure Claude Code effectively.
