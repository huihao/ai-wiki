# Meta

## Summary

Meta (formerly Facebook) is a technology conglomerate with a major AI research division (FAIR) and significant contributions to open-source AI, including the Llama family of large language models and PyTorch.

- **AI division**: FAIR (Fundamental AI Research)
- **Key AI products**: Llama models, PyTorch, Segment Anything
