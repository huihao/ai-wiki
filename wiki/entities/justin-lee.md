# Justin Seonyong Lee

## Summary

Author of "Transformers: a Primer," a math-guided walkthrough of the Transformer architecture published on his Columbia University homepage. Provides detailed derivations of Scaled Dot-Product Attention, Multi-Head Attention, and the softmax Jacobian analysis explaining the rationale for the √d_k scaling factor.
