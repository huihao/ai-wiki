# Google AI

## Summary

Google AI is the research team that developed and pioneered federated learning, enabling machine learning on decentralized data with privacy by default. The team maintains open source projects under Parfait and conducts research spanning federated learning, analytics, private aggregation, and differential privacy.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: Research organization
- **Affiliation**: Google
- **Focus**: Federated learning, privacy-preserving AI, analytics
- **Key Contribution**: Federated learning approach for deep networks (2016 paper)
- **Open Source**: Parfait projects (github.com/google-parfait)
- **Research Areas**: Federated learning, analytics, differential privacy, secure aggregation
- **Philosophy**: Private-by-default ecosystem with transparent, verifiable privacy claims
- **Applications**: Gboard, Messages, Assistant, Android Private Compute Core
