# Tony Lian

## Summary

Tony Lian is a researcher who co-led the development of ThreadWeaver, an adaptive parallel reasoning method that takes an engine-agnostic approach to parallel inference. ThreadWeaver handles orchestration on the client side, concatenating thread outputs and performing a second prefill, avoiding the need to modify inference engine internals.

## Key Attributes

- **Known for**: ThreadWeaver (co-lead)
- **Affiliation**: UC Berkeley (BAIR)
