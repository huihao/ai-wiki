# Chroma

## Definition

AI research company focused on building retrieval-augmented generation systems and vector databases. Known for ChromaDB open-source embedding database and foundational research on long-context LLM behavior.

## Key Products

**ChromaDB**: Open-source embedding database for building AI applications with LLMs. Designed to make it easy to build RAG applications with simple, developer-friendly APIs.

## Research

**Context Rot study (July 2025)**: Comprehensive evaluation of 18 LLMs demonstrating non-uniform performance degradation with increasing input length. Challenged assumption that long-context is "solved" by showing failures even on simple tasks. Extended Needle-in-a-Haystack benchmark to include semantic matching, distractors, and haystack structure analysis.

## Focus Areas

- Vector databases and embedding storage
- Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems
- Long-context LLM evaluation and behavior
- AI infrastructure optimization
