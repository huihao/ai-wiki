# Ece Kamar

## Summary

Ece Kamar is a Microsoft VP who leads Microsoft Research's AI Frontiers Lab. She was part of the team behind the Phi-3 family of small language models (SLMs), which demonstrated that smaller models trained on carefully curated, high-quality data could rival much larger LLMs on key benchmarks.

## Key Attributes

- Microsoft VP, leads Microsoft Research AI Frontiers Lab
- Part of the Phi-3 small language model development team
- Focused on AI research at Microsoft Research
