# DeepSeek

## Summary

DeepSeek is a Chinese AI company known for developing high-performing open-weight language models, particularly the DeepSeek-R1 reasoning model and the DeepSeek-V3 generalist model. It has gained attention for achieving competitive performance with efficient training approaches.

## Key Attributes

- **Origin**: China
- **Key models**: DeepSeek-R1 (reasoning), DeepSeek-V3, DeepSeek-V4
- **Approach**: Efficient training, mixture-of-experts architectures, open weights
