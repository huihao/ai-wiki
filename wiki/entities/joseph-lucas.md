# Joseph Lucas

## Summary

Joseph Lucas is a researcher on the NVIDIA AI Red Team who authored the research on improving Bash generation in small language models using grammar-constrained decoding. Developed grammargen, a tool that automatically generates Lark grammars from --help documentation or JSON tool schemas for constrained decoding.
