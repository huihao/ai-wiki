# Intel

## Summary
Intel Corporation is a multinational technology company and one of the world's largest semiconductor chip manufacturers. Intel designs and produces central processing units (CPUs), and has expanded into AI hardware accelerators (Gaudi series), GPU computing (Intel Arc/Data Center GPU), and software tools for AI inference optimization.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: Semiconductor / technology organization
- **Notable for**: Dominant x86 CPU architecture; expanding into AI accelerator hardware with Gaudi chips
- **Founded**: 1968
- **Key products**: Core and Xeon processors, Intel Gaudi AI accelerators, Intel Arc GPUs
- **Software contributions**: Intel Extension for PyTorch, OpenVINO inference toolkit, oneAPI
