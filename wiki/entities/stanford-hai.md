# Stanford HAI

## Summary

Stanford Human-Centered AI Institute (HAI) is a research institute at Stanford University dedicated to advancing AI research, education, policy, and practice to improve the human condition. HAI is known for popularizing the term "Foundation Model" in 2021 and advocating for human-centered approaches to AI development.

## Key Attributes

- **Founded:** 2019
- **Location:** Stanford University, California
- **Focus Areas:** Human-centered AI, AI policy, AI ethics, foundation models
- **Key Publications:** "On the Opportunities and Risks of Foundation Models" (2021) — the report that introduced and defined foundation models
