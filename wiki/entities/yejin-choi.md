# Yejin Choi

## Summary

Yejin Choi is a professor at the University of Washington and a senior research manager at the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence (AI2). She is known for her research on commonsense reasoning, natural language understanding, and neuro-symbolic AI. Her work focuses on enabling AI systems to understand and reason about everyday knowledge and common sense.

- **Type**: researcher / professor

- **Other facts**: professor at the University of Washington Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science; senior research manager at AI2; recipient of multiple awards for NLP research including the ACL Test of Time Award
