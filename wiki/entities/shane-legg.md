# Shane Legg

## Summary
Shane Legg is a co-founder and Chief AGI Scientist at Google DeepMind. A researcher with a long-standing focus on artificial general intelligence, he has been instrumental in shaping DeepMind's research agenda toward building AGI. He co-founded DeepMind in 2010 alongside Demis Hassabis and Mustafa Suleyman before its acquisition by Google.
