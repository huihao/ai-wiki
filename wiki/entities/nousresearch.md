# Nous Research

## Summary

Nous Research is an AI research organization known for open-source model training and the Hermes family of models. They created Hermes Agent, a self-improving AI agent with a built-in learning loop that creates skills from experience and searches past conversations.

## Key Attributes

- **Key product**: Hermes Agent (self-improving AI agent)
- **Models**: Hermes family of LLMs
- **Platforms**: Nous Portal, HermesClaw (community WeChat bridge)
- **License**: MIT
- **Community**: Discord, Skills Hub
