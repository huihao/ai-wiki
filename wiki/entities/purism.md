# Purism

## Summary

Purism is a social purpose company that develops and manufactures hardware and software focused on privacy, security, and user freedom. Known for the Librem line of laptops and the Librem 5 smartphone, Purism runs PureOS, a Debian-based GNU/Linux distribution. The company advocates for digital rights, publishes educational content on topics like reverse engineering, and has gained attention for its commitment to building privacy-respecting computing devices.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: Social purpose company (hardware and software)
- **Founded**: 2014, headquartered in Burlington, Vermont, USA
- **Notable for**: Librem 5 smartphone (one of the few privacy-focused Linux phones); hardware kill switches for camera, microphone, and wireless
- **Products**: Librem 14 (laptop), Librem Mini, Librem 5 (phone)
- **Other facts**: PureOS is one of the few operating systems endorsed by the Free Software Foundation; the company runs its own social purpose corporation (SPC) legal structure to enshrine its ethical commitments
