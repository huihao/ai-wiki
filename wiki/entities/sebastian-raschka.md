# Sebastian Raschka

## Summary

Machine learning researcher, educator, and author. Known for making complex ML/AI topics accessible through detailed blog posts, books, and open-source educational materials. His work covers LLM training, fine-tuning, and reasoning models. He publishes the "Ahead of AI" newsletter and has written *Machine Learning Q and AI* and *Build a Large Language Model (From Scratch)*.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: ML researcher / educator / author
