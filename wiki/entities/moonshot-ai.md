# Moonshot AI

## Summary

AI research lab and company known for the Kimi family of large language models. Moonshot AI has been an early adopter of Multi-Head Latent Attention (MLA) and hybrid attention architectures.

## Key Attributes

- **Known for**: Kimi K2, Kimi Linear — models using MLA and hybrid attention
- **Headquarters**: China
- **Focus**: Long-context models, efficient attention architectures
