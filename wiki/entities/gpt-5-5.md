# GPT-5.5

## Summary

GPT-5.5 is OpenAI's flagship general-purpose model released in April 2026, succeeding GPT-5.4. Described as OpenAI's "smartest and most intuitive to use model yet." Substantial benchmark gains: MRCR v2 at 1M tokens jumped from 36.6% (GPT-5.4) to 74.0%, Terminal-Bench 2.0 reaches 82.7%, hallucination rate dropped ~60% versus prior generations. Roughly 2x the per-token cost of GPT-5.4.
