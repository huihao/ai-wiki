# Yann LeCun

French-American AI researcher, Turing Award laureate (2018), and Chief AI Scientist at Meta.

## Key Contributions

- **Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)**: Developed LeNet (1989), pioneering practical image recognition with neural networks
- **Backpropagation advocacy**: One of the key researchers demonstrating backpropagation works for training neural networks in the 1980s–90s
- **Self-supervised learning**: Major advocate for pre-training approaches that don't require labeled data
- **Energy-based models**: Research on unifying framework for understanding perception and prediction

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Chief AI Scientist at Meta (formerly Facebook AI Research / FAIR)
- **Awards**: Turing Award (2018, shared with Geoffrey Hinton and Yoshua Bengio — the "Godfathers of Deep Learning")
- **Education**: PhD from Université Pierre et Marie Curie (Paris VI), postdoc with Geoffrey Hinton
