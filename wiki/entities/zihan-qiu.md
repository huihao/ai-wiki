# Zihan Qiu

## Summary

Zihan Qiu is a researcher known for contributions to attention mechanism design in large language models. She served as the lead author of "Gated Attention for Large Language Models," a NeurIPS 2025 Best Paper Award winner. The paper demonstrated that a simple sigmoid gating mechanism applied after scaled dot-product attention consistently improves performance, enhances training stability, mitigates attention sink, and enables long-context extrapolation. The approach was validated across 30 variants on 15B MoE and 1.7B dense models with 3.5T tokens and has been adopted in the Qwen3-Next model family.

## Key Attributes
- Lead author of "Gated Attention for Large Language Models" (NeurIPS 2025 Best Paper Award)
- Research areas include attention mechanisms and LLM architecture design
- Developed a widely applicable gated attention mechanism adopted by Qwen3-Next
- Work validated across 30 model variants with 3.5T training tokens
