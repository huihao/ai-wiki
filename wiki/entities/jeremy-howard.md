# Jeremy Howard

## Summary

Data scientist, researcher, and educator. Co-founder of fast.ai, where he leads development of the fastai library and teaches the Practical Deep Learning for Coders course. Former President and Chief Scientist of Kaggle. Top-ranked Kaggle competitor two years running. Founded Enlitic, the first company focused on deep learning and medicine. Advocate for making AI accessible to non-specialists.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Researcher / educator / entrepreneur
- **Notable works**:
  - fast.ai — educational organization and deep learning library
  - *Deep Learning for Coders with fastai and PyTorch* (O'Reilly)
  - Enlitic — first deep learning + medicine company
- **Previous roles**: President & Chief Scientist at Kaggle; top-ranked Kaggle competitor
- **Teaching philosophy**: Top-down learning — start with working systems, then unpack foundations
