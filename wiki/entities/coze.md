# Coze

## Summary

Conversational AI platform (by ByteDance) for building and deploying AI chatbots across multiple channels. Emphasizes no-code chatbot creation, multi-platform deployment (websites, messaging apps), and AI-powered customer engagement. Positioned as an easy entry point for businesses wanting AI chatbots without development effort.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: Chatbot automation platform
- **Parent company**: ByteDance
- **Deployment**: Cloud-only
- **Interface**: Drag-and-drop chatbot builder
- **Key features**:
  - No-code chatbot design
  - Multi-platform deployment (WhatsApp, Messenger, Slack, websites)
  - AI-powered NLP for context-aware responses
  - Automated customer support workflows
  - Multilingual support
- **Target users**: Business users, marketing teams, customer support departments
