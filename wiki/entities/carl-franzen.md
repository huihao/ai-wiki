# Carl Franzen

## Summary

Carl Franzen is a VentureBeat journalist who covered Andrej Karpathy's LLM Knowledge Base architecture -- a Markdown-based, AI-maintained wiki approach that bypasses traditional RAG complexity for mid-sized datasets. His article explained how this "file-over-app" philosophy uses LLMs to compile raw data into structured wikis with backlinks, self-healing maintenance, and synthetic data generation.
