# Benjamin Eysenbach

## Summary

Benjamin Eysenbach is a researcher known for his contributions to reinforcement learning and deep learning. He served as a senior author on "1000 Layer Networks for Self-Supervised RL," a NeurIPS 2025 Best Paper Award winner that demonstrated 1024-layer networks can significantly boost self-supervised reinforcement learning performance in goal-reaching tasks without demonstrations or rewards, overturning the conventional assumption that RL information is insufficient to train very deep networks.

## Key Attributes
- Senior author of "1000 Layer Networks for Self-Supervised RL" (NeurIPS 2025 Best Paper Award)
- Research focus includes deep reinforcement learning and self-supervised learning
- Work challenges the assumption that RL cannot effectively train deep networks
