# Google Brain

## Summary

Google Brain is the research team responsible for introducing the Transformer architecture through the landmark paper "Attention Is All You Need" in 2017. This breakthrough removed recurrence from sequence modeling and established attention as the foundational mechanism.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: AI research organization
- **Affiliation**: Google
- **Landmark Contribution**: Transformer architecture (2017)
- **Key Paper**: "Attention Is All You Need" (Vaswani et al.)
- **Innovation**: Replaced RNN/CNN recurrence with pure attention mechanisms
- **Impact**: Foundation for GPT, BERT, and modern foundation models
