# Mu Li

## Summary

Mu Li is a machine learning researcher and co-author of "Dive into Deep Learning" (D2L), an interactive, open-source deep learning textbook adopted at 500 universities across 70 countries and published by Cambridge University Press in 2023. He has been instrumental in building the multi-framework implementations (PyTorch, JAX, TensorFlow, MXNet) that make the book uniquely accessible.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Researcher, textbook author, framework developer
- **Affiliation**: Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- **Notable work**: Co-authored "Dive into Deep Learning" (D2L)
- **D2L role**: Co-author responsible for MXNet and multi-framework implementations
- **Co-authors**: Aston Zhang, Zachary C. Lipton, Alexander J. Smola
- **Framework work**: Key contributor to Apache MXNet
