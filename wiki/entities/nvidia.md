# NVIDIA

## Summary

NVIDIA is the dominant GPU manufacturer and creator of CUDA, the parallel computing platform that underpins modern deep learning and AI infrastructure. The company designs GPUs, develops CUDA and related software stacks, and provides cloud GPU instances through partnerships.

## Key Attributes

- **Founded**: 1993
- **Headquarters**: Santa Clara, California, USA
- **Key products**: GeForce (consumer GPUs), Tesla/Quadro (datacenter/professional), DGX systems, CUDA Toolkit, cuDNN, TensorRT
- **CUDA**: Parallel computing platform and programming model launched in 2006; essential for GPU-accelerated ML
- **Market position**: Dominant provider of AI training and inference hardware
