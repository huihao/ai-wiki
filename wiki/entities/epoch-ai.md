# Epoch AI

## Summary
Epoch AI is a research organization that tracks and analyzes the progress of artificial intelligence, maintaining comprehensive databases of AI benchmarks, compute trends, and training run data. It is best known for its Epoch Capability Index, which aggregates over 50 AI benchmarks into a unified capability scale covering mathematics, coding, language understanding, and agentic tasks. The organization provides independent, data-driven analysis of AI progress to inform researchers, policymakers, and the public.
