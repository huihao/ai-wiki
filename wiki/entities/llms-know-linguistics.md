# LLMs Know Linguistics

## Summary
"LLMs Know Linguistics" refers to findings from research — notably the MathArena platform at ETH Zurich — demonstrating that large language models possess implicit knowledge of linguistic structures and patterns. Evaluations on uncontaminated math and reasoning benchmarks reveal that LLMs can generalize beyond memorized training data, exhibiting understanding of abstract relational patterns that mirror linguistic competence. This research challenges the view that LLMs are mere statistical pattern matchers without genuine structural understanding.
