# Facebook

## Summary
Facebook is the flagship social media platform originally launched by Mark Zuckerberg in 2004, now operated under the parent company Meta Platforms (rebranded in 2021). The platform remains one of the most widely used social networks globally with billions of monthly active users. Meta's AI research arm, formerly FAIR (Facebook AI Research), now operates as Meta AI and is responsible for developing the LLaMA family of open-source large language models, along with contributions to computer vision, speech, and multimodal AI.
