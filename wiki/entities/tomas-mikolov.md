# Tomas Mikolov

## Summary

Tomas Mikolov is a Czech computer scientist and research scientist known for creating Word2Vec while at Google, and later developing fastText at Facebook AI Research (FAIR). His work on distributed word representations (word embeddings) was foundational to modern natural language processing, demonstrating that simple neural network models could learn meaningful semantic relationships between words.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: researcher / computer scientist
- **Notable for**: creating Word2Vec and fastText, pioneering distributed word representations
- **Other facts**: born in 1978 in Czech Republic; studied at Brno University of Technology; worked at Google Brain and later at Facebook AI Research; his 2013 Word2Vec paper is one of the most cited in NLP; fastText extends word embeddings to subword-level representations
