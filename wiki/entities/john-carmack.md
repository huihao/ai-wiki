# John Carmack

## Summary
John Carmack is a legendary game programmer, aerospace engineer, and technology executive, best known as the co-founder of id Software and the lead programmer behind iconic games such as Doom, Quake, and Wolfenstein 3D. He served as CTO of Oculus VR and has more recently turned his focus to AGI research through Keen Technologies, applying his systems-level thinking to artificial general intelligence.
