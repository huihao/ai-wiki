# Fernanda Viegas

## Summary

Fernanda Viegas is a Brazilian-American data visualization researcher and designer known for her work on interactive visualizations that make complex data accessible to broad audiences. She is a contributor to Distill and has collaborated extensively with Martin Wattenberg on projects including the TensorFlow Embedding Visualizer and research on how people interpret and interact with data visualizations.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Visualization researcher, designer
- **Affiliation**: Google (formerly), Harvard University
- **Notable work**: Co-creator of Many Eyes (IBM), TensorFlow Embedding Visualizer, Distill contributor
- **Research areas**: Information visualization, collaborative visualization, social data analysis
- **Collaborators**: Martin Wattenberg (long-time research partner)
