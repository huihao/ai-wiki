# Gabriel Goh

## Summary

Gabriel Goh is a machine learning researcher known for his work on neural network interpretability and the discovery of multimodal neurons in artificial neural networks. He is a contributor to Distill and co-authored the influential article "Multimodal Neurons in Artificial Neural Networks," which demonstrated that individual neurons in large vision-language models can respond to abstract concepts across multiple modalities.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Researcher
- **Affiliation**: OpenAI (formerly)
- **Notable work**: Lead author of "Multimodal Neurons in Artificial Neural Networks" (Distill, 2021)
- **Research areas**: Neural network interpretability, multimodal representations, feature visualization
- **Impact**: Demonstrated that CLIP neurons respond to high-level abstract concepts (not just visual features), bridging computer vision and language understanding
