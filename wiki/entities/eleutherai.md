# EleutherAI

## Summary

A decentralized collective of researchers working on open-source AI. Best known for creating the GPT-Neo and GPT-NeoX model families, as well as The Pile dataset. Pioneered open LLM research before the release of models like Llama and Mistral.

## Key Attributes

- **Type:** Decentralized research collective
- **Key Products:** GPT-Neo, GPT-NeoX, The Pile, Polyglot
- **Approach:** Open-source, reproducible AI research
