# freeCodeCamp

## Summary
freeCodeCamp is a nonprofit organization that provides free coding education through interactive browser-based lessons, projects, and verified certifications. Founded in 2014, it has grown into one of the largest open-source learning platforms for web development, with a curriculum covering HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Python, data science, and machine learning. Its open-source curriculum and active community make it a widely referenced resource for self-taught developers.
