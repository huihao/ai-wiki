# Netflix

## Summary
Netflix is a global entertainment and streaming technology company, widely recognized as one of the world's largest media services providers. Beyond streaming, Netflix is a significant contributor to machine learning and data science, particularly in recommendation systems, A/B testing at scale, and content personalization, publishing influential technical blog posts and open-source tools.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: Technology and entertainment organization
- **Founded**: 1997 (DVD rental); streaming launched 2007
- **Notable for**: Pioneer in ML-driven recommendation systems; massive-scale A/B testing; influential engineering blog
- **Scale**: Over 200 million subscribers globally
- **ML/AI contributions**: Recommendation algorithms, personalization engines, experimentation platforms
- **Open source**: Publishes tools for data science workflows (e.g., Metaflow for ML pipelines)
