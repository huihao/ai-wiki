# Business Process Automation

## Summary
Business process automation (BPA) is the use of technology to execute recurring business tasks and workflows with minimal human intervention. BPA aims to reduce manual effort, minimize errors, and accelerate process completion across areas such as data entry, approvals, reporting, and customer communications. Modern BPA increasingly integrates AI and machine learning for intelligent decision-making within automated workflows.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: concept/method
- **Notable for**: improving operational efficiency and consistency in enterprise workflows
- **Other facts**: ranges from simple rule-based automation (if-this-then-that logic) to complex AI-driven orchestration; often implemented through workflow engines, robotic process automation (RPA), or low-code platforms; increasingly complemented by AI agents capable of handling unstructured tasks
