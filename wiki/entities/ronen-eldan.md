# Ronen Eldan

## Summary

Ronen Eldan is a Microsoft Research ML expert who was inspired by reading bedtime stories to his daughter to create the "TinyStories" dataset -- a collection of millions of tiny children's stories using a vocabulary of just 3,000 carefully selected words. This breakthrough demonstrated that 10M-parameter models could produce fluent, grammatically correct narratives, paving the way for the Phi-3 family of small language models. He is a key figure in the high-quality training data approach that underpins Microsoft's SLM strategy.

## Key Attributes

- Microsoft Research ML expert
- Creator of the TinyStories dataset, inspired by his daughter's learning
- Pioneered the high-quality, curated data approach for small language model training
- Key contributor to the Phi-3 small language model family
