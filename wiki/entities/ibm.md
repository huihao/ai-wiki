# IBM

## Summary

International Business Machines Corporation. One of the oldest technology companies, now heavily invested in enterprise AI. Developed the [[ibm|IBM Granite]] model family and was the first to offer toggleable "thinking" modes in hybrid reasoning models (Granite 3.2, February 2025).

## Key Attributes

- **Founded:** 1911
- **Headquarters:** Armonk, New York
- **AI Products:** IBM Granite, Watsonx, IBM Research
- **Notable AI Hardware:** Vela AI supercomputer (thousands of Nvidia A100 GPUs)
