# James Bradbury

## Summary

Research engineer and contributor to the JAX ecosystem, particularly known for work on model scaling and efficient training systems. Co-authored the "How to Scale Your Model" book with Blake Hechtman, which systematically explains the systems-level principles for training large language models efficiently on TPUs and GPUs.
