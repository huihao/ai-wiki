# HAL Science
## Summary
An open archive platform (hal.science) for depositing and accessing scientific publications. Uses Anubis anti-bot protection with proof-of-work challenges to prevent large-scale AI scraping while maintaining open access for human researchers.
## Key Attributes
- **Type**: Open scientific archive
- **Protection**: Anubis anti-bot with proof-of-work
- **Focus**: Scientific publications and preprints
