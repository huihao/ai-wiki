# Google

## Summary

Google is a multinational technology conglomerate and one of the largest investors in artificial intelligence research and infrastructure. Its AI division, [[google|Google DeepMind]], develops frontier models including Gemini, Gemma, and Imagen. Google also created the Transformer architecture ("Attention Is All You Need", 2017), TensorFlow, JAX, and TPUs — foundational technologies for modern deep learning. Google's AI models consistently rank at the top of performance benchmarks.

## Key Attributes

- **Founded**: 1998 (AI research intensified after DeepMind acquisition in 2014 and Google Brain merger in 2023)
- **Headquarters**: Mountain View, California
- **Key AI division**: [[google|Google DeepMind]]
- **Frontier models**: Gemini, Gemma, Imagen
- **Key infrastructure**: TPUs, TensorFlow, JAX, Kubernetes, Borg
- **Notable open-source contributions**: TensorFlow, JAX, Keras, Transformers research
- **Research landmarks**: Transformer architecture (2017), AlphaGo, AlphaFold
- **Benchmark Performance**: #1 on AI leaderboard (2026) with quality score 2093
- **Context Window**: Up to 1M tokens
- **Pricing**: Competitive ($0.50-$2.50 input, $3-$15 output per million tokens)
