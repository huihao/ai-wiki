# Bowen Peng

## Summary

Bowen Peng is a researcher at Nous Research and the author of YaRN (Yet Another RoPE Extension), a method that combined NTK-by-parts interpolation with attention temperature scaling to enable LLM context window extension to 128K+ tokens with fewer than 400 training steps. YaRN represents one of the key milestones in the evolution of positional encodings that allowed LLMs to scale from 512 to 2M+ context tokens.

## Key Attributes

- Researcher at Nous Research
- Author of YaRN (Yet Another RoPE Extension)
- Contributed to the evolution of RoPE-based context window scaling techniques
