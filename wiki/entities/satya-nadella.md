# Satya Nadella

## Summary

Satya Nadella is the CEO of Microsoft, referenced in Mario Zechner's essay "Thoughts on Slowing the Fuck Down" in the context of Microsoft's 30% AI-written code correlating with Windows quality issues. Under his leadership, Microsoft has heavily invested in AI coding tools while facing questions about the quality implications of rapid AI adoption.
