# Rafi Witten

## Summary

Rafi Witten is a Google Cloud TPU/GPU Multipod tech lead who taught the High Performance LLMs 2024 course. He pioneered Accurate Quantized Training (AQT), a technique for 8-bit training on TPU v5e that reduces memory usage by 75% while maintaining model accuracy, enabling more efficient large-scale model training.

## Key Attributes

- Google Cloud TPU/GPU Multipod tech lead
- Instructor of High Performance LLMs 2024 course
- Pioneer of Accurate Quantized Training (AQT) for 8-bit training on TPU v5e
