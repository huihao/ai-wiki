# Pier-Jean Malandrino

## Summary

Pier-Jean Malandrino is CTO at SCUB and AI Ambassador for the French Ministry of Economy. He argues that the real AI coding skill is architecture, not prompting, and defines "vibe coding as abdication" — the surrender of structural decisions to a system that cannot hold the whole software reality.
