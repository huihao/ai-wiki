# Answer.AI

## Summary

AI R&D lab co-founded by [[jeremy-howard]] and [[rachel-thomas]] (also co-founders of [[nbdev-fastai|fast.ai]]). Creates practical end-user products based on foundational research breakthroughs. Known for open-source contributions including ModernBERT, rerankers, and the /llms.txt proposal.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: AI R&D lab
- **Founders**: [[jeremy-howard]], [[rachel-thomas]]
- **Philosophy**: Practical, open-source, research-informed product development
- **Notable projects**:
  - ModernBERT — SOTA encoder-only model (BERT replacement)
  - rerankers — Unified ranking methods library
  - /llms.txt — LLM web standard proposal
  - JaColBERTv2.5 — Low-resource language retrieval
  - Claudette — Literate programming tool
  - Solveit — AI fatigue antidote product
  - flexicache — Caching exploration
