# Richard Sutton

## Summary

A pioneering researcher in reinforcement learning, best known as the author of "The Bitter Lesson" (2019), an influential essay arguing that AI progress historically comes from general methods leveraging computation and data rather than human knowledge. Sutton is a Distinguished Research Scientist at DeepMind and co-author of the standard textbook "Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction."

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Distinguished Research Scientist at DeepMind
- **Famous work**: "The Bitter Lesson" (2019), Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction
- **Field**: Reinforcement learning, temporal-difference learning
- **Institution**: DeepMind (formerly University of Alberta)
