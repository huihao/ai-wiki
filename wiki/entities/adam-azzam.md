# Adam Azzam

## Summary

Adam Azzam is a colleague of Jeremiah Lowin, credited with the insightful phrase "you know a word by the company it keeps" -- a principle that captures how large language models learn word relationships from co-occurrence patterns rather than explicit definitions. This insight is central to understanding how LLMs model probability distributions over words.

## Key Attributes

- Colleague of Jeremiah Lowin, creator of the "An Intuitive Guide to How LLMs Work"
- Credited with the "company it keeps" insight about LLM word representations
