# Stability AI

## Summary

Stability AI is a London-based artificial intelligence company best known for developing Stable Diffusion, a family of open-source text-to-image generation models. Stability AI's strategy centers on open-weight release of generative models, making high-quality image, video, and audio generation accessible to researchers, developers, and artists without proprietary API costs.

## Key Attributes

- **Founded**: 2019
- **Headquarters**: London, UK
- **Flagship product**: Stable Diffusion (text-to-image), Stable Video Diffusion, Stable Audio
- **License**: Generally open-weight with commercial use permitted (varies by model version)
- **Architecture**: Latent diffusion with cross-attention for text conditioning
