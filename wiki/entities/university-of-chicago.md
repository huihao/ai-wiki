# University of Chicago

## Summary

The University of Chicago is a private research university located in Chicago, Illinois, known for its rigorous academic programs and contributions to economics, physics, and social sciences. It offers an advanced course on large language models covering interpretability, alignment, and AI agents, taught by Chenhao Tan. The course provides a deep technical survey of modern LLM research topics.
