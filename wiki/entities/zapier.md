# Zapier

## Summary

Zapier is a workflow automation platform that connects apps and services through visual, no-code integrations. Users can create automated workflows called "Zaps" that trigger actions across thousands of applications without writing code. The platform supports hundreds of integrations and is widely used in business process automation.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: workflow automation platform (SaaS)
- **Notable for**: no-code integration platform connecting 6,000+ apps through automated workflows
- **Other facts**: founded in 2011 by Wade Foster, Bryan Helmig, and Mike Knoop; headquartered in San Francisco; supports multi-step Zaps, conditional logic, and data formatting; widely adopted in SMB automation
