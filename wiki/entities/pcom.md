# PCOM

> Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine — Institution providing AI in healthcare education resources.

## Overview

Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine (PCOM) is a medical school that maintains educational resources on AI applications in medicine and healthcare through its LibGuides platform.

## AI Medicine Resources

Maintains comprehensive guide on "Artificial Intelligence in Medicine and Healthcare" covering:

- **RSS Feeds** — 10+ journals and PubMed searches for AI medical literature
- **Journals** — JAMA AI, Nature Machine Intelligence, Radiology AI
- **Databases** — Medical informatics collections
- **Datasets** — AI healthcare datasets
- **Guidelines** — Policy and standards for AI in medicine
- **Research** — Methodology and best practices
- **Publishing** — Guidance for AI medical research publication
- **Training** — Online courses in medical AI

## Purpose

Educational resource for:
- Medical students learning AI applications
- Researchers conducting AI healthcare studies
- Clinicians implementing AI tools
- Healthcare institutions evaluating AI solutions
