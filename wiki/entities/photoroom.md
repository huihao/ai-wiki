# Photoroom

## Summary

AI-powered photo editing company that trained a text-to-image foundation model from scratch, developing the PRX (Photoroom eXperimental) architecture. Their open-source project benchmarks DiT, MMDiT, DiT-Air, U-ViT, and PRX architectures for text-to-image generation, with PRX achieving the best efficiency-quality trade-off.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: AI company / photo editing platform
- **Project**: PRX (Photoroom eXperimental) — open-source T2I model
- **Key contribution**: PRX architecture with text token caching for inference efficiency
- **Text encoder**: Adopted T5Gemma 2B as drop-in replacement for T5-XXL
- **Autoencoder**: Released checkpoints with DC-AE (×32 compression)
