# lm-provers

## Summary

lm-provers is a research organization focused on theorem proving with language models. They demonstrated that small models (4B parameters) can prove Olympiad-level mathematical theorems, challenging assumptions about model scale requirements.

## Key Achievements

- **QED-Nano**: 4B parameter model for Olympiad-level proof generation
- Demonstrated efficiency of specialized training for mathematical reasoning
- Proved hard mathematical theorems without trillion-parameter models

## Key Attributes

- **Focus**: Mathematical theorem proving with LLMs
- **Models**: QED-Nano (4B parameters)
- **Platform**: Hugging Face Spaces for demonstrations
