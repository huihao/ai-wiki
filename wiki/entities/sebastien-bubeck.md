# Sebastien Bubeck

## Summary

Sebastien Bubeck is Microsoft's VP of Generative AI Research who led the small language model (SLM) efforts resulting in the Phi-3 family. Under his leadership, the team demonstrated that smaller models trained on carefully curated, high-quality data could outperform models twice their size on language, coding, and math benchmarks, challenging the prevailing assumption that scale is the primary driver of model capability.

## Key Attributes

- Microsoft VP of Generative AI Research
- Led the Phi-3 small language model development effort
- Champion of the high-quality data approach for compact model training
