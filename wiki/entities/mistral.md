# Mistral

## Summary
Mistral AI is a French artificial intelligence company founded in 2023 by former Meta and Google DeepMind researchers. It has quickly become one of Europe's most prominent AI labs, releasing competitive open-weight and open-source large language models including the Mistral, Mixtral, and Pixtral families, along with the Mistral Large proprietary model.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: AI company / organization
- **Founded**: 2023
- **Headquarters**: Paris, France
- **Notable for**: Leading European AI lab; pioneer of efficient MoE (Mixture of Experts) models
- **Key models**: Mistral 7B, Mixtral 8x7B, Mixtral 8x22B, Mistral Large, Pixtral, Codestral
- **Open-source contributions**: Several open-weight models that became benchmarks for efficiency and performance
- **Founders**: Arthur Mensch, Timothee Lacroix, Guillaume Lample (ex-Meta and DeepMind researchers)
