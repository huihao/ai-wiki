# Piotr Niedzwiedz

## Summary

Founder and CEO of Neptune.ai, an experiment tracking and model monitoring platform for machine learning. Neptune.ai was acquired by OpenAI in December 2025 to strengthen OpenAI's training infrastructure and research tooling capabilities.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Founder and CEO of Neptune.ai
- **Focus**: ML experiment tracking, model monitoring, training workflow analysis
- Built Neptune into a leading experiment tracking platform providing researchers with tools to analyze training workflows, compare thousands of runs, and understand complex model behavior in real-time
- OpenAI acquired Neptune.ai to integrate its experiment tracking capabilities into OpenAI's training stack
