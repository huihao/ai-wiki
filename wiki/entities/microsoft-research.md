# Microsoft Research

## Summary
Microsoft Research (MSR) is Microsoft's research division, conducting foundational and applied research across computer science, AI, mathematics, and related fields. Established in 1991, MSR has produced influential work in machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and systems research, with labs worldwide.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: Research organization / division
- **Parent**: [[microsoft]]
- **Notable for**: Foundational contributions to deep learning, speech recognition, and NLP; development of GPT models (via partnership with OpenAI)
- **Founded**: 1991
- **Labs**: Research labs in Redmond, Cambridge (MA), Cambridge (UK), Beijing, Bangalore, and other locations
- **Key contributions**: Deep learning framework research, Phi language model series, DeBERTa, Turing-NLG
