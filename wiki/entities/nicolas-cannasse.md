# Nicolas Cannasse

## Summary

Nicolas Cannasse is a developer and creator of Pico GPU, a 300KB memory GPU designed for learning, experimentation, and shader-based creative projects. His work focuses on educational GPU programming environments and innovative uses of GPU technology for both graphics and sound synthesis.
