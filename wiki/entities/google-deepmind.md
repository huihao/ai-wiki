# Google DeepMind

## Summary

Google DeepMind is Google's AI research division, formed from the merger of Google Brain and DeepMind in 2023. It develops frontier AI models including Gemini, Gemma, and Imagen, and conducts foundational research in reinforcement learning and protein folding (AlphaFold).

## Key Attributes

- **Formed**: 2023 (merger of Google Brain and DeepMind)
- **Key products**: Gemini, Gemma, Imagen, AlphaFold
- **Parent**: Alphabet / Google
