# Stephen Wolfram

## Summary

British-American computer scientist, mathematician, and businessman. Founder and CEO of Wolfram Research, creator of [[what-is-chatgpt-doing-wolfram|Mathematica]], [[what-is-chatgpt-doing-wolfram|Alpha]], and the [[large-language-models|Wolfram Language]]. Known for his work on cellular automata, computational complexity, and the [[the-evolution-of-modern-rag-architectures|Principle of Computational Equivalence]]. Author of the influential 2023 essay "[[what-is-chatgpt-doing-wolfram|What Is ChatGPT Doing … and Why Does It Work?]]"

## Key Attributes

- **Born:** 1959, London, England
- **Notable Works:** A New Kind of Science (2002), Wolfram Language, Mathematica
- **Companies:** Wolfram Research (founder/CEO)
- **Fields:** Computer science, mathematics, physics, complexity theory
