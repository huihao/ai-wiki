# Junyang Lin

## Summary

Junyang Lin is a senior researcher on the Qwen team at Alibaba, known for contributions to large language model architecture and training. He served as a senior author on "Gated Attention for Large Language Models," a NeurIPS 2025 Best Paper Award winner that demonstrated a simple sigmoid gating mechanism applied after scaled dot-product attention consistently improves performance, enhances training stability, mitigates attention sink, and enables long-context extrapolation. The approach was validated across 30 variants on 15B MoE and 1.7B dense models with 3.5T tokens and has been adopted in the Qwen3-Next model family.
