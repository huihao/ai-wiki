# Yu Wu

## Summary

Yu Wu is a researcher specializing in 3D vision and multi-view attention mechanisms. Lead author of RayRoPE (Projective Ray Positional Encoding for Multi-view Attention), a novel positional encoding scheme for transformers in multi-view settings.
