# Luca Antiga

## Summary

Luca Antiga is a machine learning engineer and researcher who played a key role in the early development of PyTorch. He led the refactoring of LuaTorch's C backends (TH/THC, THNN/THCUNN) to be language-agnostic, a critical prerequisite that enabled the creation of PyTorch as a Python-based framework. He later co-founded Orchestra AI and contributed to deep learning infrastructure.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: ML engineer, framework architect
- **Affiliation**: Orchestra AI (co-founder), formerly at multiple ML organizations
- **Notable work**: Led backend refactoring for PyTorch's creation
- **Key contribution**: Refactored LuaTorch C backends to be language-agnostic (2015-2016), enabling PyTorch's Python-based design
- **Collaborators on refactoring**: Andreas Kopf, Sergey Zagoruyko, Soumith Chintala, Adam Paszke, Francisco Massa
