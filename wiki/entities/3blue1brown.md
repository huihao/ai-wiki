# 3Blue1Brown (Grant Sanderson)

## Summary

3Blue1Brown is the mathematics YouTube channel created by Grant Sanderson, known for its distinctive visual style explaining mathematical concepts through animation. The channel's visualizations of concepts like ellipses from lines and modulo multiplication have inspired interactive educational content on Amit Patel's Red Blob Games website, demonstrating the cross-pollination between math education creators.

## Key Attributes

- Mathematics YouTube channel (youtube.com/3blue1brown)
- Created by Grant Sanderson
- Known for visual, animated explanations of mathematical concepts
- Inspired interactive demos on Red Blob Games (ellipse from lines, modulo multiplication)
