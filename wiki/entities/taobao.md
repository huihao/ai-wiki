# Taobao

> Chinese e-commerce platform, part of Alibaba Group, with significant AI engineering and automation practices.

## Overview

Taobao (淘宝) is a major Chinese online shopping platform owned by Alibaba Group. The platform's engineering teams have developed advanced AI automation systems for operational efficiency, particularly in data governance, ticket processing, and baseline operations.

## AI Engineering Practices

### Automation Framework
- **Perception-Decision-Execution Loop**: Complete AI automation methodology
- **Three Automation Patterns**: Tool assistant, complex workflow, intelligent agent
- **Hierarchical Agent Architecture**: Multi-level agent systems for complex problem-solving

### Production Systems
- **Data Storage Governance**: AI-driven table lifecycle management, dependency analysis
- **Ticket Auto-Operations**: Agent-based ticket processing and troubleshooting
- **Baseline Operations**: Automated monitoring, alerting, and dependency management
- **Impact Analysis**: Automated upstream change impact assessment across thousands of tasks

### Results
- Thousands of tables processed through AI governance
- Manual review of 10% high-priority samples: zero误删 errors
- Full automation of baseline monitoring and recovery
- 86% resolution time reduction in race-day issue resolution (F1 AWS case study, similar practices)

## Engineering Team

**商家 & 开放平台技术团队** (Merchant & Open Platform Technical Team):
