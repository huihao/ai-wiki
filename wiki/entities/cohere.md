# Cohere

## Summary
Cohere is a Canadian AI company focused on building enterprise-grade large language models and multilingual embeddings. Founded by former Google Brain researchers, Cohere specializes in retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), semantic search, and text classification for enterprise use cases. Its Command model family and Embed API are designed for production deployment with emphasis on data privacy, since models can be deployed on-premise or in private clouds.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: AI company
- **Notable for**: enterprise-focused LLMs; multilingual embeddings; RAG-optimized models
- **Founded**: 2019
- **Headquarters**: Toronto, Ontario, Canada
- **Key products**: Command (text generation), Embed (embeddings), Rerank (search re-ranking)
