# Ivan Velichko (iximiuz)

## Summary
Ivan Velichko, known online as iximiuz, is a DevOps educator and author behind iximiuz.com. He is known for creating the "Kubernetes 101" workshop that teaches Kubernetes concepts by first demonstrating how deployments were done with VMs, then showing how Kubernetes automates those same patterns. He maintains interactive labs at labs.iximiuz.com for hands-on learning.
