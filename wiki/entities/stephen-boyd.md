# Stephen Boyd

## Overview

Stanford University professor and co-author of "Introduction to Applied Linear Algebra – Vectors, Matrices, and Least Squares". Expert in optimization, control systems, and applied mathematics.

## Key Contributions

- Author of fundamental linear algebra textbook used at Stanford and UCLA
- Development of ENGR108 course at Stanford
- Research in convex optimization, control theory, machine learning
- Free educational resources for applied mathematics
