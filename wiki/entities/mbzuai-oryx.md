# MBZUAI-Oryx

## Summary

Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) Oryx Lab is a research group focused on computer vision, multimodal learning, and LLM reasoning. They authored the comprehensive survey "LLM Post-Training: A Deep Dive into Reasoning Large Language Models" and maintain the Awesome-LLM-Post-training repository.

## Key Work

- **Awesome-LLM-Post-training** — curated collection of post-training papers, benchmarks, frameworks
- **LLM Post-Training Survey** — taxonomy of fine-tuning, RL, test-time scaling (arXiv:2502.21321)
