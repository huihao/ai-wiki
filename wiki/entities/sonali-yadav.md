# Sonali Yadav

## Summary

Sonali Yadav is a Microsoft Principal Product Manager for Generative AI who was part of the team behind the Phi-3 family of small language models (SLMs). The Phi-3 project demonstrated that smaller, more efficient models could rival much larger LLMs on key benchmarks through innovative training on high-quality, carefully curated data.

## Key Attributes

- Microsoft Principal Product Manager for Generative AI
- Part of the Phi-3 small language model development team
