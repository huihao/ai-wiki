# IBM Granite

## Summary
IBM Granite is a family of enterprise-focused large language models developed by IBM Research. The Granite series includes hybrid reasoning variants with toggleable thinking modes, designed for deployment in enterprise environments with emphasis on trust, transparency, and responsible AI.

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: Large language model family
- **Developed by**: [[ibm-research]]
- **Notable for**: Enterprise-grade LLMs with hybrid reasoning capabilities and configurable thinking modes
- **Open weights**: Granite models have been released as open-weight models on platforms like Hugging Face
- **Focus areas**: Business automation, code generation, and domain-specific enterprise tasks
