# OpenAI

## Summary

OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company known for developing GPT models, ChatGPT, DALL-E, and Codex. It has shifted from a non-profit to a capped-profit structure and maintains a strategic partnership with Microsoft. OpenAI's models are widely used and rank among top performers in AI benchmarks, holding multiple top-20 positions.
