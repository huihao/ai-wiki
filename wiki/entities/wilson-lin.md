# Wilson Lin

## Summary

Wilson Lin is the author of the "From Kevin Bacon to HNSW" series and developer of CoreNN, an open source vector database for querying across billions of vectors on commodity machines. His work focuses on making vector search practical at scale.

## Key Attributes

- **Role**: Developer, author, researcher
- **Project**: CoreNN (github.com/wilsonzlin/corenn)
- **Expertise**: Vector search, ANN indexing, billion-scale systems
- **Key Achievement**: 1 billion Reddit embeddings in 15ms from 4.8TB index
- **Approach**: Commodity machine deployment, cost optimization
- **Blog**: blog.wilsonl.in
- **Series**: 3-part deep dive into vector search (HNSW, DiskANN, CoreNN)
