# Frank Rosenblatt

## Summary

American psychologist and AI pioneer who invented the perceptron in 1957, one of the earliest neural network models. The perceptron remains a foundational concept in machine learning, still used and referenced in modern neural network education.

## Key Attributes

- Invented the perceptron (1957)
- Cornell University researcher
- Created the Mark I perceptron machine
- Pioneer of connectionism and neural network research
