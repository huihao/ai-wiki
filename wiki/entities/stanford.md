# Stanford University

## Summary

Stanford University is a leading private research university located in Stanford, California. It is home to the Stanford Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (SAIL), one of the most influential AI research groups in history. Stanford has been central to many foundational advances in AI, machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing, and its alumni and faculty have founded or led organizations including Google, OpenAI, DeepMind, and many others.

## Key Attributes

- **Type**: Private research university
- **Founded**: 1885; opened 1891
- **Notable for**: Stanford AI Lab (SAIL); deep ties to Silicon Valley; outsized influence on AI research and the tech industry
- **Location**: Stanford, California, USA
- **Other facts**: Notable AI figures include Andrew Ng, Fei-Fei Li (ImageNet), Daphne Koller, Percy Liang (HELM), and many more; publishes influential research through CS229, CS231n, CS224n, and other courses; home to HAI (Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence Institute)
