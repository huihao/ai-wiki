# Mozilla

## Summary
Mozilla is a global nonprofit organization dedicated to keeping the internet open, accessible, and beneficial to all people. Best known for creating the Firefox web browser, Mozilla also develops privacy-focused tools, advocates for internet policy, and funds open-source projects through initiatives like Mozilla Common Voice (voice data) and Mozilla.ai (trustworthy AI).

## Key Attributes
- **Type**: Nonprofit technology organization
- **Founded**: 2005 (as a spinoff from Netscape/AOL)
- **Notable for**: Firefox browser, privacy advocacy, open-source commitment
- **Key products**: Firefox, Firefox Monitor, Mozilla VPN, Common Voice dataset
- **Mission**: Ensure the internet remains a global public resource, open and accessible to all
- **License**: Most projects are open-source (MPL)
