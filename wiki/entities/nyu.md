# New York University (NYU)

## Summary

New York University is a major private research university and a leading institution in AI/ML research, home to Yann LeCun (Turing Award winner, Chief AI Scientist at Meta) and the Center for Data Science.

## Key Attributes

- Private research university in New York City
- Center for Data Science: leading AI/ML research hub
- Home to Yann LeCun (Turing Award 2018, Chief AI Scientist at Meta)
- NYU Tandon School of Engineering for CS and cybersecurity
- Contributions to deep learning, computer vision, and NLP
