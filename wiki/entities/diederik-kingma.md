# Diederik Kingma

Dutch AI researcher and co-inventor of the Adam optimizer, one of the most widely used optimization algorithms in deep learning.

## Key Contributions

- **Adam optimizer (2014)**: Co-invented with Jimmy Ba; combines momentum with adaptive learning rates. Default optimizer for most deep learning tasks
- **AdamW**: Extension decoupling weight decay from gradient-based updates
- **VAE (Variational Autoencoder)**: Co-invented with Max Welling (2013/2014); foundational generative model
- **Glow**: Normalizing flow model for high-fidelity image generation

## Key Attributes

- **Education**: PhD from University of Amsterdam (2016)
- **Affiliation**: Previously OpenAI, Google Brain; researcher in probabilistic generative models and optimization
