# HashiCorp

## Summary

Infrastructure automation company co-founded by Mitchell Hashimoto and Armon Dadgar. Known for products including Terraform (infrastructure as code), Vault (secrets management), Consul (service mesh), and Nomad (workload orchestration). Co-founder Mitchell Hashimoto coined the term "Harness Engineering" to describe infrastructure for managing AI agents.

## Key Attributes

- **Founded**: 2012
- **Co-founders**: Mitchell Hashimoto, Armon Dadgar
- **Key products**: Terraform, Vault, Consul, Nomad, Vagrant
- **Public**: IPO 2021 (HCP), acquired by IBM in 2024
