# Evaluation Metrics

## Definition

Evaluation metrics are standardized measurements used to assess the performance of AI systems, including RAG applications. Metrics measure accuracy, speed, relevance, and other quality indicators.

## Key Proponents / Critics

- Research community developing benchmark standards
- Industry practitioners establishing production metrics

## Evolution

Evaluation metrics for RAG systems include retrieval precision, answer accuracy, latency, and user satisfaction, evolving from simple accuracy measures to multi-dimensional quality assessments.
