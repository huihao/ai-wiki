# Recommender Systems

## Definition

Recommender systems are machine learning algorithms that predict and suggest items of interest to users based on their preferences, behavior, and the preferences of similar users. They are a core application area in deep learning, spanning collaborative filtering, content-based methods, and hybrid approaches, and are covered comprehensively in foundational deep learning education.

## Key Aspects

- One of the key application domains in modern deep learning alongside computer vision, NLP, and reinforcement learning
- Covered as a major topic in interactive deep learning textbooks (e.g., Dive into Deep Learning)
- Techniques include collaborative filtering, matrix factorization, and neural collaborative filtering
- Deep learning approaches enable learning complex non-linear user-item interaction patterns
- Systems must balance relevance, diversity, novelty, and serendipity in recommendations
- Evaluation involves metrics like precision, recall, NDCG, and AUC-ROC
- Production systems face challenges of scale, cold-start problems, and real-time serving
