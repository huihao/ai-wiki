# The Pile

## Definition

A large, diverse, open-source language modeling dataset constructed by EleutherAI in 2020 and made publicly available on December 31 of that year.

## Key Characteristics

- Composed of 22 smaller datasets
- Includes 14 new datasets created specifically for this collection
- Designed for training large language models
- Open-source and publicly available

## Purpose

Used as training data for foundation models and large language models, providing diverse text sources for model training.
