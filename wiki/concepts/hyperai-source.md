# HyperAI Source

## Definition

HyperAI Source refers to the HyperAI platform (hyper.ai), an AI workflow platform offering notebooks, datasets, GPU compute, and state-of-the-art benchmarking -- positioned as a comprehensive ecosystem for AI development from idea to launch under the motto "Build AI with AI."

## Key Aspects

- Provides free AI co-coding notebooks with out-of-the-box environments and competitive GPU pricing
- Maintains an AI dataset repository for training and research
- Publishes daily-updated cutting-edge AI research papers
- Tracks 3,900+ tasks, 73,000+ benchmarks, and 18,000+ papers across the SOTA (State of the Art) index
- Serves as a unified platform for discovering, comparing, and advancing state-of-the-art models
- Part of the broader AI workflow platform ecosystem alongside tools like R2R and knowledge graph systems
