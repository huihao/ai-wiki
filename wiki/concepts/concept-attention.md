# ConceptAttention

## Definition

ConceptAttention is a technique for interpreting and visualizing generative models by analyzing attention patterns to understand what concepts the model has learned and how it uses them during generation. Developed by Alec Helbling at Georgia Tech, it provides tools for making the internal reasoning of diffusion models and other generative systems more transparent to humans.

## Key Aspects

- **Attention Visualization**: Reveals which concepts and spatial regions the model attends to during generation
- **Generative Model Interpretability**: Makes the internal operations of diffusion models understandable
- **Research Origin**: Developed at Georgia Tech under advisor Polo Chau, published as arXiv 2025
- **Companion Work**: Diffusion Explorer (interactive diffusion model visualization), ManimML (ML animation), PrefGen (guiding model behavior)
- **Industry Applications**: Applied at Apple (Responsible AI, vision-language models), Adobe Firefly (text-to-image safety)
- **HCI Focus**: Bridges ML research with human-computer interaction for explanation tools
- **Practical Value**: Helps developers understand why models generate specific outputs, enabling debugging and improvement
