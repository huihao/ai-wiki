# RAG benchmarking

## Definition

Standardized evaluation of Retrieval-Augmented Generation systems across retrieval quality (recall@k, MRR, NDCG), generation quality (faithfulness, relevance, completeness), and end-to-end metrics (answer accuracy, hallucination rate). Includes benchmarks like RAGAS, RGB, and CRAG for measuring how well retrieval augmentation improves LLM outputs.
