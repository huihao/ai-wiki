# Hierarchical RL

Reinforcement learning with multiple policy levels: a high-level (meta) policy makes abstract decisions while low-level (sub) policies handle concrete actions. Used in HDPO where the meta-policy decides whether to use a tool and the sub-policy selects which tool.
