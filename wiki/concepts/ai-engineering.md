# AI Engineering

## Definition

The discipline of building production systems with large language models (LLMs) and other foundation models. Distinct from AI research (training new models) and traditional ML engineering (feature engineering, tabular models). AI Engineering focuses on prompting, RAG, agents, evals, finetuning, and orchestration of existing models into useful products.

## Evolution

The term "AI Engineering" gained traction in 2023–2024 as a way to describe engineers who build with LLMs without necessarily training them. It sits at the intersection of software engineering, ML ops, and product design. As models improve, the discipline shifts from prompt hacking toward more systematic approaches: eval-driven development, agent orchestration, and compound AI systems.
