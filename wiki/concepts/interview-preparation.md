# Interview Preparation

## Definition

Interview preparation for AI and software engineering roles encompasses systematic practice across coding challenges, system design, and domain-specific knowledge to demonstrate technical competency and problem-solving ability during the hiring process.

## Key Aspects

- LeetCode-style algorithm practice builds fluency in data structures, time/space complexity analysis, and coding under time constraints
- System design interviews test ability to architect scalable, distributed systems -- covering load balancing, caching, databases, and API design
- AI/ML-specific preparation includes understanding model architectures (transformers, attention mechanisms), training methodologies, and inference optimization
- Career development strategies include building a portfolio of projects, contributing to open source, and networking within the AI community
- Effective preparation balances breadth (general CS fundamentals) with depth (specialized ML knowledge)
- Mock interviews and structured study plans improve performance more than unstructured practice
