# Scan and Solve

## Definition

Scan and Solve is an AI-powered interaction pattern where users capture an image of a problem (typically mathematical equations or formulas) and the system automatically recognizes, parses, and solves it with step-by-step explanations. It combines computer vision (OCR for mathematical notation) with symbolic computation and language model reasoning to provide accessible, on-demand problem-solving.

## Key Aspects

- Users upload or photograph a problem image, and the system extracts mathematical content via OCR
- AI interpretation converts visual notation into machine-readable mathematical expressions
- Step-by-step solution generation provides educational value beyond just the final answer
- Platforms like Julius AI serve over a million students with free math assistance
- The pattern extends beyond math: any visual problem that can be OCR'd and reasoned about qualifies
- Accuracy depends on image quality, notation clarity, and the complexity of the mathematical expressions
- Integration with broader data analysis platforms enables context-aware problem solving
- Accessibility focus makes expert-level problem solving available without human tutors
