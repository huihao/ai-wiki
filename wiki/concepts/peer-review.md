# Peer review

## Definition

The evaluation of scientific work by other experts in the same field before publication or acceptance. In academic publishing, peer review serves as a quality control mechanism to ensure research meets field standards before publication.

## Evolution

OpenReview promotes an open peer review model where reviews, author responses, and discussions are publicly visible rather than hidden. This contrasts with traditional closed peer review systems where reviews are confidential. The open model aims to increase transparency, accountability, and fairness in the review process.
