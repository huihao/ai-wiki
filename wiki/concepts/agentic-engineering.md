# Agentic Engineering

## Definition

An engineering paradigm where humans act as coordinators rather than code authors, using systematic context management, knowledge沉淀, and process design to enable AI agents to work autonomously while maintaining quality. Coined by Andrej Karpathy in February 2026 as the disciplined successor to "vibe coding."

Agentic Engineering is not Vibe Coding's opposite but its工程化演进 — preserving the leverage of "let AI do the work" while adding quality assurance and knowledge compounding.
