# Portfolio Projects

## Definition
Portfolio projects are tangible demonstrations of technical skill and knowledge that engineers and ML practitioners build to showcase their capabilities to employers or collaborators. They serve as evidence of practical ability, problem-solving approach, and domain expertise beyond what resumes or credentials alone can convey.

## Key Aspects
- Provide concrete evidence of skills that complement resumes, credentials, and interview performance
- Help bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application
- Particularly important in ML/AI fields where demonstrating end-to-end project completion is valued
- Should show progression in complexity and depth over time
- Best portfolio projects solve real problems rather than following toy tutorials
- Include clear documentation, reproducible setups, and references to relevant source material
