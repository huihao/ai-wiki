# AI Timeline

## Definition
AI timeline refers to the systematic tracking and visualization of AI development milestones, model capabilities, and historical events from early AI research through the current era of large language models. It provides context for understanding the pace of AI advancement and projecting future developments toward artificial general intelligence.

## Key Aspects
- Dr. Alan D. Thompson's LifeArchitect.ai serves as a "gold standard" for post-2020 AI capability tracking, used by Microsoft, governments, and AI labs
- Quarterly retrospectives since mid-2021 ("The sky is..." series) document the accelerating pace of AI development
- Historical milestones include: AI fire alarm (Jul 2021), AI outperforming humans (Sep 2021), leaders guilty of negligence (Oct 2023)
- Major model families tracked include GPT series, Gemini, Grok, o1/o3, Nova, and emerging Chinese models
- Emphasis on readiness for rapid advancement: superintelligence will solve current concerns almost immediately
