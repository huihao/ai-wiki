# Scientific Communication

## Definition

Scientific communication refers to the dissemination, exchange, and discussion of scientific knowledge through publications, conferences, presentations, and other venues. It encompasses both formal publication channels and informal scholarly discourse.

## Evolution

Platforms like OpenReview are shifting scientific communication toward greater transparency by making peer review discussions publicly visible. This openness allows broader participation in scientific discourse and enables the community to learn from review processes, critiques, and author responses. The trend represents a move away from opaque, closed-door peer review toward more transparent scholarly communication.
