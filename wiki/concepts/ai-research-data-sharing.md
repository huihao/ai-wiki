# AI Research Data Sharing

## Definition

AI research data sharing is the practice of contributing user interaction data from AI platforms to advance AI research and model improvement. Platforms like LMArena explicitly disclose that conversations and certain personal information may be shared with AI providers and the research community to support model evaluation and development.

## Key Aspects

- Crowdsourced benchmarking relies on user interactions to generate evaluation data
- Conversations and personal information may be disclosed to AI providers and publicly shared
- Users are advised not to submit sensitive information they would not want shared publicly
- The data sharing model creates a tension between research utility and user privacy
- Shared data enables qualitative assessment beyond what controlled benchmarks can measure
- Transparency about data practices is essential for ethical research platforms
