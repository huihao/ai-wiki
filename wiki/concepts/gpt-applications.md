# GPT Applications

## Definition

GPT Applications are software applications built on top of GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) models, leveraging their language understanding and generation capabilities to perform tasks across text, image, audio, and productivity domains. Platforms like WaytoAGI aggregate thousands of GPT-powered applications, categorizing them by function and providing a central hub for discovery.

## Key Aspects

- **Application Categories**: Text generation, image creation, video production, audio processing, and productivity tools — each leveraging GPT models differently
- **API Marketplace**: GPT applications are distributed through API marketplaces and skill directories, enabling developers to integrate language capabilities into their own products
- **Chinese AI Ecosystem**: WaytoAGI (通往AGI之路) serves as the largest Chinese AI knowledge base, aggregating thousands of GPT applications and tools
- **Rapid Proliferation**: The accessibility of GPT APIs has led to rapid creation of applications across industries, from education to enterprise
- **Daily Curation**: Platforms provide daily knowledge selections featuring the latest GPT-based research, tools, and insights
