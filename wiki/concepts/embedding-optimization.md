# Embedding Optimization

## Definition

Embedding optimization refers to techniques for improving the quality and efficiency of embeddings—numerical representations of data used for semantic search and similarity matching.

## Key Proponents / Critics

- RAG system developers and researchers

## Evolution

Embedding optimization techniques include dimensionality reduction, quantization, domain-specific training, and hybrid approaches combining sparse and dense representations.
