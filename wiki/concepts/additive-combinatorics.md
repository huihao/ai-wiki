# Additive Combinatorics

Branch of combinatorics studying the additive structure of sets, particularly sumsets (A+A, hA), restricted sumsets, and related structures like Sidon sets. Key questions involve characterizing achievable sumset sizes and bounding the diameter needed to realize them.
