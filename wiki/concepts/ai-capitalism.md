# AI Capitalism

## Definition

AI Capitalism refers to the increasing corporate and investment firm dominance in AI and NLP research, characterized by privatization of scientific ideas, safeguarding of intellectual property, and market-driven control over innovation trajectories. This trend raises concerns about the impact on open scientific collaboration and the direction of AI development.

## Key Proponents / Critics

- **Belinda Li** — critical analysis in "Large Language Models, Innovation, and Capitalism"
- Academic researchers concerned about open science erosion

## Related Concepts

- Innovation Dynamics — how corporate control shapes scientific progress
- Research Privatization — safeguarding vs. open collaboration
- LLM Industry — economic concentration in large language model development
- Corporate Research Labs — industry dominance over academic AI research

## Evolution

- 2010s: Academic AI research dominated by universities
- 2020s: Shift toward corporate labs (Google, Meta, OpenAI, Anthropic)
- 2023+: Critical examination of privatization trends and innovation impacts
