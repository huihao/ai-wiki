# Academic publishing

## Definition

The system through which research results are disseminated to the academic community, typically via journals and conferences. In AI/ML research, conference proceedings (particularly through platforms like [[openreview|OpenReview]]) have become primary publication venues.

## Evolution

In AI/ML fields, conference publications have become more prestigious than journal publications, with major conferences like NeurIPS and ICML serving as the primary venues for cutting-edge research. OpenReview represents a shift toward transparent peer review where reviews and author responses are publicly visible, changing the traditionally closed peer review model.
