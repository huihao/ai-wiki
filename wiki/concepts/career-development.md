# Career Development

Strategic approach to advancing one's professional trajectory through deliberate skill building, networking, and opportunity seeking.

## Two Modes of Action

### Strategic Actions
High-effort, high-return, compound advantages:
- Learning new technologies deeply via substantial portfolio projects
- Building and maintaining professional network
- Creating personal brand through public work
- Long-term tenure at reputable organizations

### Tactical Actions
Low-effort, high-return in specific situations:
- Company research before interviews
- LeetCode problem practice for muscle memory
- Mock interviews for preparation
- CV polish and formatting

## Strategic Career Framework

Based on [[max-mynter-research-engineer-career|Max Mynter's 18-month journey]]:

### 1. Goal Definition
Define specific criteria for ideal role:
- Career inflecting vs. lateral move
- Skill development trajectory
- Impact and ownership level
- Growth support from senior peers
- Geographic and lifestyle constraints

### 2. Skill Gap Analysis
Identify missing skills from job descriptions:
- Technical foundations (CS fundamentals, distributed systems)
- Domain-specific expertise (LLMs, ML systems)
- Portfolio gaps (publications, OSS contributions)

### 3. Strategic Upskilling
Close gaps with measurable projects:
- Open-source contributions (merged PRs to high-profile repos)
- Publications (peer-reviewed or conference presentations)
- Portfolio projects with business outcomes
- Cohort-based programs for intensive learning

### 4. Network Building
- Twitter/X for domain-specific relationships
- Cohort programs for committed peers
- Friends-of-friends for warm introductions
- Conferences, meetups, technical events

### 5. Application Strategy
- Batch applications with mixed risk levels
- Referrals for top-choice roles
- Insider information via network outreach
- Multiple simultaneous processes for leverage

## Career Capital

Building rare and valuable skills with verification:
- Degrees and credentials
- Tenure at reputable organizations
- Portfolio artifacts (code, presentations, publications)
- Network endorsements and referrals

- **Platforms**: LeetCode, NeetCode for interview prep
