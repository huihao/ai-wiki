# Vibe Coding

## Definition

A style of AI-assisted programming where the developer "fully gives in to the vibes, embraces exponentials, and forgets that the code even exists" (Andrej Karpathy, Feb 2025). Characterized by unreviewed AI output, no formal specs, and shipping when it "looks like it works." By early 2026, Karpathy and the industry had largely moved to the more disciplined "agentic engineering."
