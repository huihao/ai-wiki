# Security and Compliance

## Definition

Security and compliance in AI systems refers to requirements for protecting data, meeting regulatory standards, and ensuring safe deployment in enterprise environments.

## Key Proponents / Critics

- Enterprise compliance officers, security teams, regulatory bodies
