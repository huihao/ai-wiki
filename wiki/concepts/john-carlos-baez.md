# John Carlos Baez

## Definition

John Carlos Baez is an American mathematical physicist and science communicator known for making abstract mathematical and physical concepts accessible to a broad audience through his blog, talks, and educational content. His work on topics ranging from quantum gravity to category theory has influenced creators of interactive educational resources, including Amit Patel's Red Blob Games.

## Key Aspects

- Mathematical physicist at UC Riverside, known for writing about mathematical physics, combinatorics, and environmental topics in an accessible style
- His blog "Azimuth" and earlier "n-Category Café" have been influential in the mathematical physics and science communication communities
- Referenced as an inspiration for Red Blob Games' educational interactive visualizations, specifically the orbits of planets demos
- Advocates for visual and geometric intuition as a path to understanding abstract mathematics
- His exposition style emphasizes building understanding from first principles, using diagrams and examples before formalism
- Bridging the gap between research mathematics and public understanding is a central theme of his work
