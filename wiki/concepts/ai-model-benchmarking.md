# AI Model Benchmarking

## Definition

AI Model Benchmarking is the systematic evaluation of large language models across standardized metrics including quality, speed, cost, and task-specific performance. Benchmarks provide objective data for model selection and performance comparison.

## Evolution

The proliferation of AI models has created need for transparent, objective benchmarks. Modern benchmarks track multiple dimensions: quality scores, inference speed, token pricing, context windows, and licensing terms. This enables evidence-based model selection for different use cases.
