# AI Education

## Definition
AI education encompasses the use of AI-powered tools and platforms to enhance learning, as well as the broader effort to teach AI concepts and skills to students, developers, and researchers. It includes AI math solvers, curated knowledge bases, and community-driven resource aggregations that make AI more accessible across language barriers.

## Key Aspects
- AI-powered math solvers (e.g., Julius AI) provide step-by-step solutions from uploaded images, used by over a million students
- Knowledge bases like WaytoAGI aggregate thousands of AI tools, research papers, and tutorials across categories (text, image, video, audio)
- Chinese AI knowledge bases bridge Western and Eastern AI communities, translating and curating daily AI research and tool discoveries
- Educational platforms combine practical implementation guides (Claude Code setup, cursorrules) with theoretical resources
- Growing emphasis on accessible, free educational tools that reduce barriers to AI literacy
