# AI Visualization

## Definition

AI visualization is the practice of creating visual representations of AI model capabilities, architectures, and performance to make complex AI concepts accessible and trackable. Dr. Alan D. Thompson's LifeArchitect.ai exemplifies this through comprehensive visualizations of post-2020 AI model capabilities and development timelines.

## Key Aspects

- Dr. Thompson maintains dedicated visual pages for major LLM families (GPT, Gemini, Grok, Claude)
- Quarterly "The sky is..." retrospectives provide longitudinal visualization of AI progress biannually
- Deep-dive analyses like "What's in GPT-5?" make model capabilities concrete and comparable
- Visualizations serve both technical researchers and policy/government audiences
- LifeArchitect.ai is described as the "gold standard" for AI capability visualization
- Microsoft collaborates with Thompson for AI papers, lending institutional credibility
- Visualization helps bridge the gap between raw benchmark numbers and human understanding
