# Distill Publication Approach

## Definition

Distill pioneered an approach to machine learning communication that emphasizes interactive, visual explanations with embedded experiments and working code. The publication philosophy centers on interpretability and understanding rather than performance metrics alone.

## Core Principles

- **Interactive format**: Articles are not static text but include interactive visualizations and experiments
- **Visual communication**: Heavy emphasis on diagrams, animations, and visual explanations
- **Executable content**: Code and experiments embedded directly in articles
- **Interpretability focus**: Understanding why and how models work, not just what they achieve
- **Open access**: No paywalls, freely available to all readers

## Notable Examples

- **Activation Atlases**: Interactive maps of what neural networks have learned
- **Feature Visualization**: Understanding neuron and layer behavior through optimization
- **Attention Mechanisms**: Visual guide showing how attention works in transformers
- **t-SNE Guide**: When and why dimensionality reduction visualizations can mislead
- **GAN Artifacts**: Checkerboard patterns arising from deconvolution

## Impact

Distill influenced how ML research is communicated, demonstrating that complex concepts become accessible through careful visual and interactive explanation. The "building blocks" approach to interpretability techniques showed how combining methods creates powerful understanding tools.
