# Inference Cost

The per-request cost of running LLM inference in production. Determined by model size, sequence length, hardware, and optimization techniques. At scale, a 30% reduction in inference cost can save tens of millions of dollars annually.

## Unit Economics Context

Per-unit revenue versus cost analysis for determining business viability. In AI products, inference cost is the dominant per-unit expense, creating a structural challenge where costs scale like infrastructure while revenue scales like software.
