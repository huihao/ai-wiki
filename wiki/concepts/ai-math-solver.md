# AI Math Solver

## Definition

AI Math Solver refers to AI-powered systems that can solve mathematical problems from uploaded images or text, providing step-by-step solutions. These systems use computer vision and language models to interpret problems and generate educational explanations.

## Evolution

AI math solvers represent the application of computer vision and language models to educational technology. By combining image recognition with symbolic math understanding and natural language generation, these systems can provide accessible, instant mathematical assistance. The debate continues around whether such tools support learning or bypass educational development.
