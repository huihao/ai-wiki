# AI Knowledge Base

## Definition

An AI knowledge base is a centralized repository that aggregates, curates, and organizes AI-related information, tools, research, and resources for easy discovery and use. WaytoAGI (通往AGI之路) exemplifies this as the largest Chinese AI knowledge base, serving as a hub for AI enthusiasts, researchers, and developers.

## Key Aspects

- Aggregates thousands of AI tools across categories: text, image, video, audio, and productivity
- Provides daily curated knowledge selections featuring latest AI research, tools, and insights
- Categorizes tools by function: infrastructure, chatbot, image generation, video creation, audio, and productivity
- Features AI agents, prompts, and application showcases alongside educational resources
- Bridges Western AI innovations with Chinese developer communities through translated content
- Emphasizes practical implementation over theoretical discussion
- Serves multiple audiences simultaneously: enthusiasts, researchers, and production developers
