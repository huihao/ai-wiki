# ImageNet Dataset

Landmark image database organized according to WordNet hierarchy (currently only nouns), instrumental in advancing computer vision and deep learning research.

## Structure

Each node of hierarchy depicted by hundreds and thousands of images. Organized by WordNet noun categories.

## Historical Impact

Instrumental in advancing computer vision and deep learning research. Key role in 2012 deep learning revolution (AlexNet breakthrough).

## Availability

Free to researchers for non-commercial use.
