## Summary

Dr. Alan D. Thompson's LifeArchitect.ai is a comprehensive resource for understanding, visualizing, and tracking the capabilities of post-2020 AI models. It provides regular summaries of major LLM developments (GPT series, Gemini, Grok, o1/o3, etc.), visualizations of AI capabilities, and forward-looking analyses of the AI revolution. Thompson has authored over 100 papers and articles, 300+ videos, and publishes regular editions of "The Memo" — insights consumed by major AI labs, governments, and intergovernmental bodies.

## Key Takeaways

- **Authority & reach**: Microsoft collaborates with Thompson for AI papers; LifeArchitect.ai is described as the "gold standard" for post-2020 AI capability tracking.
- **Comprehensive model coverage**: Maintains dedicated pages for major LLM families including:
  - Apple LLMs, Chinchilla, ERNIE, GPT-3.5 through GPT-6, Gemini, Grok, Nova, o1/o3/o4-mini
  - Historical milestones: AI fire alarm (Jul/2021), AI outperforming humans (Sep/2021), leaders guilty of negligence (Oct/2023)
- **Deep-dive analyses**: Specialized content like "What's in Grok?", "What's in GPT-5?", "The psychology of modern LLMs", "What's in my AI?", and "Endgame"
- **Quarterly retrospectives**: "The sky is..." series tracking AI progress biannually since mid-2021 (on fire, bigger, infinite, entrancing, comforting, quickening, steadfast, delivering, supernatural)
- **Vision for superintelligence**: Thompson articulates a optimistic vision: remove everything negative from life, multiply everything positive. Technology is already improving daily since 2020; rollout is just steps behind. "Don't waste time trying to find faults with superintelligence that will be solved (by superintelligence) almost immediately anyway."
