# ImageNet (source)

## Metadata

- **Author**: Fei-Fei Li et al. (Stanford, Princeton)
- **Date**: 2009–2021
- **URL**: https://www.image-net.org/
- **Fetched**: 2026-04-24

## Summary

Large-scale image database organized by WordNet hierarchy. ~14 million images across ~21,000 categories. Instrumental in the deep learning revolution following AlexNet's victory in the 2012 ILSVRC competition.

## Key Takeaways

- The "ImageNet moment" of 2012 proved deep CNNs could dramatically outperform traditional computer vision methods
- Remains the standard pretraining dataset for vision models
- Free for non-commercial research use
- Organized using WordNet noun hierarchy
