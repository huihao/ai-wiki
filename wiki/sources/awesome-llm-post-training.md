## Summary

A curated repository and survey paper on LLM post-training methodologies for reasoning. Covers fine-tuning, reinforcement learning (PPO, DPO, GRPO), reward modeling, test-time scaling, and reasoning benchmarks. Associated with the paper "LLM Post-Training: A Deep Dive into Reasoning Large Language Models."
