## Summary

Comprehensive comparison of three automation and AI platforms: n8n (workflow automation), Dify (AI application development), and Coze (AI chatbot creation). Each serves distinct needs with different technical requirements.

## Key Takeaways

- **n8n**: Open-source workflow automation, self-hosted or cloud, extensive API integrations, best for developers and technical users
- **Dify**: AI-native platform for LLM-powered applications, low-code/no-code, cloud-based, ideal for enterprises integrating AI
- **Coze**: Conversational AI platform, no-code chatbot builder, multi-platform deployment, perfect for customer support automation
- **Workflow automation**: n8n excels; Dify and Coze are AI-centric
- **AI integration**: Dify and Coze have native LLM support; n8n uses third-party APIs
- **Deployment**: n8n offers self-hosting; Dify and Coze are cloud-only
