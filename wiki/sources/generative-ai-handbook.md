## Summary

A comprehensive learning roadmap for generative AI, covering foundational topics often omitted from deep learning crash courses. Organized into sections on statistical prediction, time-series analysis, online learning/regret minimization, reinforcement learning, and Markov models — all with curated resource links.

## Key Takeaways

- **Statistical prediction**: Random variables, regression/classification, linear models, bias-variance tradeoffs, empirical risk minimization.
- **Time-series analysis**: Autoregressive models, ARIMA as mental model for LLMs, stochastic differential equations for diffusion.
- **Online learning/regret minimization**: Points arrive sequentially, low average error; connections to Adam (AdaGrad roots) and GANs.
- **Reinforcement learning**: MDPs, policies, trajectories; essential for RLHF and agent applications.
- **Markov models**: Markov chains, MCMC, Hidden Markov Models; path-independence assumption central to many ML methods.
