## Summary

Distill was a machine learning publication focused on clear, interactive explanations of ML concepts with emphasis on interpretability, visualization, and understanding. After five years of operation, the editorial team announced a hiatus in 2021. The publication pioneered interactive article formats combining mathematics, visualizations, code, and discussions.

## Raw Notes

Distill pioneered the "interactive article" format that has influenced how ML research is communicated. The emphasis on visualization and understanding rather than just performance metrics reflected a philosophical stance that ML should be a science, not just engineering. The publication's hiatus in 2021 leaves a gap in the ML communication landscape, though its archive remains valuable.

The editorial note "Science is a human activity. When we fail to distill and explain research, we accumulate a kind of debt..." captures the publication's ethos — research communication is essential, not optional.
