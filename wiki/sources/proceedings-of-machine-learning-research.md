## Summary

PMLR is an open-access series that publishes machine learning research presented at conferences and workshops. Each volume is separately titled and associated with a particular event. Authors retain copyright.

## Key Takeaways

- ISSN: 2640-3498.
- Publishes proceedings for ML conferences and workshops (ICML, AISTATS, CoRL, etc.).
- Originally known as JMLR W&CP; rebranded in April 2017.
- Maintains a reissue series for republishing volumes no longer online.
