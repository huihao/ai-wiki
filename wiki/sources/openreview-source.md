## Summary

OpenReview is an open-access platform for peer review and publication of scientific papers, widely used in machine learning and AI. It hosts conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, etc.) with transparent or double-blind review processes.

## Key Takeaways

- Supports open, transparent, and double-blind peer review workflows.
- Hosts major ML conferences: NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ACL, CVPR, and many others.
- Features: paper submission, reviewer assignment, discussion forums, meta-reviews, decision letters.
- Authors can respond to reviews during the discussion period.
- Accepted papers are often publicly accessible with full review history.
- Also supports workshops, journals, and stand-alone submissions.
