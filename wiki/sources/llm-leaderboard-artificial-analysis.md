## Summary

A comprehensive LLM comparison leaderboard ranking 100+ models across intelligence, output speed, latency, price, and context window. Updated with models as of early 2026.

## Key Takeaways

- GPT-5.5 (xhigh) ranks #1 on intelligence index (score 60), followed by GPT-5.5 (high) and Claude Opus 4.7 (max).
- Mercury 2 is the fastest model at ~677 tokens/sec.
- Qwen3.5 0.8B is the cheapest at $0.02 per 1M tokens.
- Llama 4 Scout and Grok 4.20 support the largest context windows (up to 10M).
- Kimi K2.6 is the highest-ranked open-weights model (intelligence score 54).
- The leaderboard distinguishes reasoning vs. non-reasoning models and open vs. proprietary weights.
