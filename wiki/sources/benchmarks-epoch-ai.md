## Summary

A searchable index of 50+ AI benchmarks covering mathematics, coding, agentic action, reasoning, and real-world tasks. Epoch AI aggregates evaluations into a single general capability scale.

## Key Takeaways

- Covers diverse domains: research-level math (FrontierMath), coding (SWE-bench, Exercism), agentic action (OSWorld, computer-use), commonsense reasoning (HellaSwag, ARC), and multimodal video QA.
- Includes emerging benchmarks for long-context modeling, cybersecurity agents, and CLI-based model post-training.
- Useful for comparing frontier model capabilities across standardized tasks.
