## Summary

Free textbook on applied linear algebra fundamentals, focusing on vectors, matrices, and least squares methods. Used as the primary textbook for Stanford's ENGR108 and UCLA's EE133A courses. Provides practical foundations for machine learning, data science, and engineering applications.
