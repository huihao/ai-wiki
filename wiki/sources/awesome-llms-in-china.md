# Awesome LLMs in China

## Summary

A comprehensive curated list of large language models developed in China. Covers 100+ models from tech giants, startups, universities, and research labs across multiple domains and modalities.

## Key Takeaways

- **Scale**: 100+ documented Chinese LLMs as of 2023–2024.
- **Key players**:
  - **Tech giants**: Baidu (文心一言), Alibaba (通义千问/Qwen), Tencent (混元), ByteDance (豆包/云雀), Huawei (盘古), JD (言犀), Xiaomi (MiLM)
  - **AI labs**: Zhipu AI (ChatGLM/清言), Baichuan Intelligence (百川), 01.AI (Yi), Moonshot AI / [[deepseek-sparse-attention|DeepSeek]] (深度求索), MiniMax (ABAB), SenseTime (商量)
  - **Universities**: Tsinghua (ChatGLM lineage), Peking University (MOSS, ChatLaw, CodeShell), Fudan, Zhejiang University, Chinese Academy of Sciences
- **Vertical domains**: General, code, medical, legal, financial, education, government, transportation, agriculture, media, scientific research.
- **Base architectures**: Many models are based on LLaMA (Meta) or BLOOM, with Chinese-specific fine-tuning and SFT/RLHF.
- **Open-source availability**: Roughly half of listed models are open-source (✔) vs. closed (✘).

## Raw Notes

- The list demonstrates the rapid proliferation of LLMs in China, with significant government, academic, and corporate investment.
- Many models are derivative of Western base models (LLaMA, BLOOM), raising questions about independent capability vs. adaptation.
- The vertical specialization (medical, legal, financial, education) is more pronounced than in the Western ecosystem, possibly due to regulatory and language-specific requirements.
- This resource is useful for understanding the competitive landscape that [[deepseek-sparse-attention|DeepSeek]] operates within.
