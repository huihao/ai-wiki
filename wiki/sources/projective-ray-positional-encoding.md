Research paper introducing RayRoPE, a novel positional encoding scheme for multi-view attention mechanisms. Published as arXiv preprint arXiv:2601.15275.

## Key Takeaways

- Proposes RayRoPE for encoding positional information via projective rays in multi-view settings.
