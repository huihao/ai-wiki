## Summary

Prime Intellect provides compute and infrastructure for training, evaluating, and deploying agentic models. Offers hosted RL training, evaluations, serverless/dedicated inference, and an environments hub for agentic workflows.

## Key Takeaways

- Hosted RL training with managed infrastructure and integrated environments.
- Evaluations platform for benchmarking model performance.
- Deployment: dedicated or serverless inference with custom LoRA adapter support.
- Environments Hub: hundreds of open-source RL environments.
- Verifiers library: modular components for RL environments and LLM agent training.
- Compute marketplace: on-demand 1-256 GPUs, multi-node clusters up to 5000+ GPUs.
- Supports SLURM, Kubernetes, Infiniband, and Grafana monitoring.
