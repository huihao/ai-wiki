## Summary

A curated directory of large language models (LLMs) created by John Rush (@johnrushx). The directory helps LLM developers showcase their models and assists those searching for exceptional large language models. Lists LLMs with the biggest models at the top, organized to help AI enthusiasts and experts discover available models. Offers monthly email updates with new additions and provides options for boosting/publishing models through the platform.

## Key Takeaways

- **Directory purpose**: Showcase developed LLMs and help users find exceptional models
- **Organization**: Biggest LLMs listed at the top
- **Updates**: Monthly email updates with new additions (no spam)
- **Publishing options**: Can publish tool immediately with forever do-follow link, or wait for publication with no-follow link
- **Boosting options**: Contact via Twitter @johnrushx with GPT URL, boost type, price, and timing preferences
- **Timeline**: Approximately 3 months to add new tools (for free publication path)
