## Summary

WaytoAGI (通往AGI之路, "Road to AGI") is a comprehensive Chinese AI knowledge base platform aggregating thousands of AI websites, tools, GPT applications, AI agents, and industry news. The platform serves as a central hub for AI enthusiasts, researchers, and developers, providing curated daily knowledge selections, tool categories, and resources to support the journey toward artificial general intelligence.

## Key Takeaways

- Largest and most comprehensive Chinese AI knowledge base platform
- Aggregates thousands of AI tools across categories: text, image, video, audio, productivity
- Provides daily curated knowledge selections featuring latest AI research, tools, and insights
- Categorizes tools by function: infrastructure, chatbot, image generation, video creation, audio, productivity
- Features AI agents, prompts, and application showcases
- Includes API marketplace and educational resources (养虾白皮书)
- Serves multiple audiences: AI enthusiasts, researchers, and developers
- Regular updates with latest AI developments and industry news

## Raw Notes

The platform demonstrates China's active engagement with AI tool adoption and knowledge sharing. Unlike Western platforms focused on individual tools, WaytoAGI aggregates and categorizes comprehensively. Daily selections feature translated content from major Western sources (Sequoia, Every, Lenny's Podcast) alongside Chinese developments (DeepSeek, Hermes Agent). The platform bridges Western AI innovations with Chinese developer communities, providing practical guides (Claude Code setup, cursorrules collections) and enterprise tools (EverMemOS, KoalaQA). Notable emphasis on practical implementation over theoretical discussion.
