## Summary

The Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR) is a premier open-access journal in machine learning, established in 2000. It provides an international forum for high-quality scholarly articles across all areas of ML. All papers are freely available online. Final versions are published electronically immediately upon receipt. Paper volumes are published by Microtome Publishing.

## Key Takeaways

- **Open-access pioneer**: Founded in 2000 as one of the earliest fully open-access ML journals; all papers freely available online.
- **Rapid publishing**: Final versions published electronically immediately upon acceptance.
- **Current volume**: Volume 27 began in 2025; Volume 26 completed March 2026.
- **Scope**: Covers all areas of machine learning — theory, algorithms, applications, and open-source software.
- **Special issues**: Includes dedicated issues such as the 2022 special issue on climate change.
- **Open source software track**: Publishes papers on ML open-source software (e.g., DifferentiationInterface.jl, UQLM, skwdro).
- **Editorial history**: Bernhard Schölkopf served as co-Editor-in-Chief until 2021.
