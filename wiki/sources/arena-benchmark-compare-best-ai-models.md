# Arena: Benchmark and Compare the Best AI Models

## Metadata

## Summary

Landing page for LMArena, a platform enabling side-by-side comparison and benchmarking of frontier AI models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other top LLMs). The platform provides crowdsourced benchmarks and leaderboards, allowing users to chat with multiple AI models simultaneously and compare their performance. Users can submit queries and receive responses from different models, with conversations and personal information potentially disclosed to AI providers and publicly shared to support community and AI research.

## Key Takeaways

- Platform purpose: Chat with multiple AI models side-by-side for direct comparison
- Supported models: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other top-tier LLMs
- Key features: Crowdsourced benchmarks, leaderboards, multi-model chat interface
- Research contribution: User conversations support AI research community
- Privacy considerations:
  - Conversations and certain personal information disclosed to relevant AI providers
  - Information may otherwise be disclosed publicly
  - Users advised not to submit sensitive information they wouldn't want shared publicly
- Usage terms: Continuing to use services acknowledges and directs platform to engage in sharing
- Brand positioning: "Experience the frontier" — emphasis on frontier AI models
- Platform maturity: Operational platform with active model comparison features

## Raw Notes

The landing page emphasizes user experience ("Experience the frontier") while clearly disclosing data sharing practices. Users must accept that conversations are shared with AI providers and potentially publicly disclosed. This transparency is important for research-oriented platforms: user interactions contribute to model evaluation and improvement. The platform's core value is enabling direct comparison—users can see how different models respond to the same query, facilitating qualitative assessment beyond numerical benchmarks. Privacy disclosure suggests the platform prioritizes research utility over user privacy; users advised explicitly not to submit sensitive information.
