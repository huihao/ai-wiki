## Summary

A meta-overview of ZenML's LLMOps database containing 457 real-world case studies as of January 2025. Summarizes patterns across RAG, agents, prompt engineering, evaluation, performance optimization, and security.

## Key Takeaways

- Database grew from ~300 to 457 entries by January 2025, representing 600,000+ words of implementation details.
- Thematic deep dives cover: RAG, advanced search/retrieval, LLM agents, prompt management, evaluation, performance/cost optimization, and security.
- Common production challenges: domain knowledge management, retrieval optimization, conversation flow design, latency, regulatory compliance.
- Real-world deployments span banking (customer support chatbots), gaming (toxic speech detection), healthcare (natural language querying), and developer documentation.
