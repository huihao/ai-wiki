## Summary

The official leaderboards for SWE-bench, a benchmark for evaluating language models on real-world software engineering tasks. Tracks performance across Verified, Multilingual, Lite, and Multimodal subsets.

## Key Takeaways

- SWE-bench Verified: 500 human-filtered instances.
- SWE-bench Multilingual: 300 tasks across 9 programming languages.
- SWE-bench Lite: lower-cost evaluation subset.
- SWE-bench Multimodal: issues with visual elements.
- mini-SWE-agent scored 65% on Verified in 100 lines of Python (07/2025).
- SWE-agent 1.0 is open-source SOTA on SWE-bench Lite.
