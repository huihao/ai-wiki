# Roboflow Universe

Roboflow Universe is the world's largest collection of open source computer vision datasets and APIs.

## Platform Statistics

- 1 billion+ images
- 1 million+ datasets
- 250,000+ fine-tuned models

## Key Features

### Dataset Categories

- Object detection datasets
- Instance segmentation datasets
- Classification datasets
- Pre-trained models for various tasks

- Football players detection (372 images, 15 models)
