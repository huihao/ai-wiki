## Summary

Academic homepage of Mehryar Mohri, Professor of Computer Science at NYU and Research Scientist at Google. Lists recent papers on rational transductors, LLM self-improvement, modular learning, optimization algorithms, and game-theoretic learning.

## Key Takeaways

- Recent work spans augmenting Transformers with Weighted Automata (Rational Transductors), principled LLM self-improvement, and modular LLM learning.
- Co-authored papers with Corinna Cortes and Jon Schneider on modular learning and calibration.
- Developed new optimization algorithms (Pion and Leon) for nonsmooth nonconvex problems.
- Won Best Paper Award at EC 2025 for work on swap regret beyond normal-form games.
- Also teaches courses and tutorials at NYU.
