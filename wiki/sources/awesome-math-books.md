## Summary

A curated collection of classic mathematics textbooks spanning foundations, probability, analysis, geometry, linear algebra, and problem-solving. Emphasizes Russian/Soviet-era textbooks known for rigorous, conceptual depth (Kiselev, Gelfand, Kolmogorov, Pontryagin, etc.) and includes modern ML-relevant texts.

## Key Takeaways

- **Classical Russian texts**: Kiselev's Arithmetic (125+ years in print, 100M+ copies), Kiselev's Algebra, Markov's Probability.
- **Core analysis**: Rudin's Principles, Kolmogorov & Fomin's Introductory Real Analysis, Nikolsky's Course of Mathematical Analysis.
- **Linear algebra**: Gantmacher's Theory of Matrices, Shilov's Linear Algebra, Halmos's Problem Book.
- **Probability**: Feller, Gnedenko, Markov, Lamperti, Alon & Spencer (probabilistic method).
- **ML-relevant**: Deisenroth et al.'s Mathematics for Machine Learning, Bishop's PRML, Murphy's PML, Shalev-Shwartz & Ben-David's UML.
- **Philosophy**: focus on deep conceptual understanding, logical reasoning, minimal bloat.
