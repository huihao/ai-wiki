# LibGuides - Artificial Intelligence in Medicine and Healthcare: AI RSS Feeds

> Source: [PCOM LibGuides](https://libguides.pcom.edu/ai_medicine/rss_feeds)

## Summary

A curated collection of RSS feeds providing valuable insights into the evolving landscape of AI in healthcare. Maintained by Heidi Nance at Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine (PCOM), this guide aggregates recent articles from leading journals, blogs, and collections focused on AI applications in medicine.

## RSS Feeds Included

### Journals

1. **JAMA+ AI** — Journal of the American Medical Association AI section
2. **JAMA Network Special Topic: Artificial Intelligence** — AI-focused research papers
3. **Intelligence-Based Medicine** — Journal on AI-driven medical decision-making
4. **International Journal of Medical Informatics** — Medical information systems
5. **Artificial Intelligence in Medicine** — AI methodologies in healthcare
6. **Nature Machine Intelligence** — High-impact AI research journal
7. **BioData Mining** — Data mining and analysis for biomedical research
8. **BMC Bioinformatics** — Open access bioinformatics research
9. **Radiology - Artificial Intelligence** — AI in medical imaging
10. **Journal of Medical Information Research (JMIR) AI** — Medical informatics and AI

### Search Feeds

- **PubMed - Artificial Intelligence** — Search feed for AI-related medical literature using MeSH headings:
  - Artificial Intelligence
  - Machine Learning
  - Natural Language Processing AND Medicine
  - Medical Informatics
  - Health Services Research

### Organizations

- **Google AI Health** — Google's health AI initiatives and research

## Structure

The guide is organized into multiple sections:
- **Home** — Overview and introduction
- **The Why of AI in Medicine** — Motivation and rationale
- **AI New and Noteworthy** — Recent developments
- **AI RSS Feeds** — Current feed collection
- **AI Journals** — Journal directory
- **AI eBooks** — Book resources
- **AI Databases** — Database collections
- **AI Datasets** — Dataset compilations
- **AI Streaming Media** — Video and audio resources
- **AI Guidelines** — Policy and standards documents
- **AI Research** — Research methodology
- **AI Publishing** — Publication guidance
- **AI Online Training** — Educational courses

## Purpose

Provides researchers, clinicians, and students with a centralized resource for tracking latest developments in AI-driven healthcare solutions, medical informatics, and clinical decision support systems.
